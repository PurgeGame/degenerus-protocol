<objective>
Replace the Phase 1 carryover ETH distribution state machine with single-pass ticket distribution in Phase 0. Delete the 3 storage vars that supported the multi-call ETH loop, remove the DailyCarryoverStarted event, change the carryover budget from 50% (DAILY_REWARD_JACKPOT_LOOTBOX_BPS=5000) to 0.5% (50 bps) of futurePrizePool, and update AdvanceModule to remove dailyEthPhase references.

Purpose: Simplify the daily jackpot carryover from a resumable ETH distribution state machine into a single-pass ticket purchase. The Phase 1 ETH loop existed because ETH distribution could exceed gas limits; ticket distribution is cheaper and fits in one pass.

Output: Modified JackpotModule, Storage, and AdvanceModule with all Phase 1 carryover machinery removed.
</objective>
