<objective>
Audit all 11 `_creditClaimable` call sites across 5 files to confirm or deny that every invocation is paired with a `claimablePool +=` in the same execution path (either inline Pattern A or batched liabilityDelta Pattern B). Produce the ACCT-02 verdict.

Purpose: ACCT-02 is the most likely unconfirmed HIGH/CRITICAL finding in the protocol. The function `_creditClaimable` updates only `claimableWinnings[beneficiary]` and does NOT update the aggregate `claimablePool`. If any call site credits a player without updating `claimablePool`, the ETH solvency invariant is broken — the contract will eventually hold less ETH than it owes.

Output: ACCT-02 verdict (PASS or finding with severity) with per-call-site classification, written to 08-01-SUMMARY.md.
</objective>
