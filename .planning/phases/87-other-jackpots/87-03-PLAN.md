<objective>
Audit the decimator jackpot mechanics -- both the regular decimator (burn-based bucket/subbucket system with deferred claims at levels x5 and x00) and the terminal decimator (death bet resolved at GAMEOVER). This is the most stateful of the "other jackpots" with per-player DecEntry structs, bucket aggregates, packed winning subbucket storage, and activity-score-based multipliers.

Purpose: The decimator has complex state management (per-player burn tracking, bucket migration, pro-rata claims) and a potential storage collision between regular and terminal variants sharing decBucketOffsetPacked. This audit ensures all state transitions, claim paths, and edge cases are correctly understood.
