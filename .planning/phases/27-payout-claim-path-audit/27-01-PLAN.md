**PAY-16: Ticket Conversion and Futurepool Mechanics**

The audit MUST cover:

1. **_budgetToTicketUnits** (JackpotModule:1096-1135): Verify the conversion formula that turns an ETH budget into ticket units. Verify the 2x over-collateralization: tickets are priced at `ticketPrice(lvl+1)` (next level's price) to ensure the pool can always cover redemption. Verify no rounding exploitation: `ethSpent = ticketCount * ticketPrice` must not exceed the budget.

2. **_distributeLootboxAndTickets**: Verify the function correctly allocates the lootbox portion of jackpot winnings. Trace the flow: ETH budget -> ticket conversion -> lootbox distribution -> remainder handling. Verify the ticket count and ETH spent are correctly tracked.

3. **Pool transition chain**: Verify the 4-pool transition: `futurePool -> nextPool -> currentPool -> claimablePool`. Trace when each transition occurs (at level advancement). Verify no funds are lost or duplicated during transitions. Verify `prizePoolFrozen` correctly prevents mutations during jackpot phase.

4. **Ticket pricing**: Verify `_ticketPrice(level)` or `ticketPriceAtLevel(level)` returns the correct price for each level. Cross-reference against v1.1-level-progression.md. Verify the pricing at level+1 (next level) is used for over-collateralization.

5. **Auto-rebuy carry interaction**: Verify that when auto-rebuy converts winnings to tickets, the ticket pricing uses the correct level, and the over-collateralization is maintained. Verify the carry mechanism: unconverted ETH remainder from auto-rebuy is credited as claimable.

6. **Extraction analysis**: Verify no path allows a player to extract more ETH than the pool contains. Specifically verify: (a) ticket conversion cannot create more value than the ETH input, (b) over-collateralization prevents insolvency at level transitions, (c) auto-rebuy bonus (130%/145% for afKing) is funded by protocol incentive, not accounting error.
