**Recommendation: pick (3), implemented as a foil-specific Degenerette ETH resolver.**

Do **not** change `_distributePayout`’s frozen branch. Option (1) violates (i): regular frozen ETH Degenerette wins would become observably capped/spilled instead of receiving the current full `ethShare` when `pendingFuture` is sufficient.

Best contained fix: add `resolveFoilEthSpinFromBox(...)`, have foil ETH claims call that selector, and in that resolver only, when `prizePoolFrozen`, compute the normal spin payout but skip `_distributePayout`; emit the spin with `claimableEth = 0` and recirculate the full `payout` as lootbox value.

Why it satisfies the constraints:

- (i) Regular Degenerette behavior unchanged: `_distributePayout` and existing `resolveEthSpinFromBox` stay behavior-identical.
- (ii) Foil ETH lane cannot drain uncapped `pendingFuture`: frozen foil ETH pays `0` ETH, which is stricter than the 10% cap, and spills all value to lootbox.
- (iii) No pending-pool liveness dependency: foil claims no longer revert on insufficient `pendingFuture` and do not need a future unfreeze to settle.

Guard location: **Degenerette resolve entry**, not raw `_payFoilTier`. FoilPack does not have the private Degenerette payout math; putting the guard there would either duplicate logic or route `stake` instead of actual `payout`.
