**FINDINGS & VERIFICATIONS (STORAGE-01..07)**

**(STORAGE-01 / FC-389-02) Silent narrowing truncation on segregated ETH / pools**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** The property holds because the economic/physical bounds strictly constrain the values far below the narrowed widths. In `StakedDegenerusStonk.sol`, `INITIAL_SUPPLY` is `1e30`, which fits comfortably within `uint128.max` (3.4e38), guaranteeing `poolBalances` cannot overflow. `_pendingRedemptionEthValue` is capped by the real-world ETH supply (~1.2e26 wei), which is orders of magnitude below `uint96.max` (7.9e28 wei). Truncation is perfectly safe.

**(STORAGE-02) Masked read-modify-write helper preserves its co-resident field(s)**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** The masked helpers strictly preserve sibling data. For example, `_setLootboxEvUsedFor` shifts the `windowA` block by 88 bits when replacing `windowB`, clearing precisely the 88 bits of Window B via `_EV_WINDOW_B_MASK` (`(1 << 88) - 1 << 88`), leaving Window A perfectly untouched. 

**(STORAGE-03) Cross-module readers/writers use identical shift/mask conventions**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** Modules universally inherit `DegenerusGameStorage` rather than declaring their own copies, ensuring identical layouts. Delegatecalled libraries and module-side unpackers (like `_unpackLootbox`) strictly abide by the centralized `LB_AMOUNT_MASK`, `LB_ADJ_MASK` constants.

**(STORAGE-04 / FC-389-01) Two-window EV-cap eviction under resolve-cursor lag**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** A third distinct live key never exists, making the two-window eviction perfectly safe. Every deposit path (`DegenerusGameMintModule`, `DegenerusGameWhaleModule`, and `GameAfkingModule`'s cover-box) writes to `level + 1`. Correspondingly, every deferred open path (`resolveAfkingBox`, `resolveLootboxDirect`, `_resolveRedemptionChunk`) rolls from the live level and calls `_applyEvMultiplierWithCap` with `currentLevel = level + 1`. Because delayed afking boxes draw from the *live* level at the exact moment of opening (e.g. they draw from the `13` bucket if opened when `level = 12`), they harmonize precisely with new deposits. The code strictly limits writes to `{level + 1}` at any given instant.

**(STORAGE-05) Privatized/packed fields keep external ABI getters**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** All packed states maintain their original explicit unpacker getters to preserve ABI compatibility across the trust boundary.

**(STORAGE-06 / FC-389-04) Stale-slot test harness**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** Aside from the known legacy `RedemptionInvariants.inv.t.sol` harness, the current active test harnesses correctly reflect the post-shift slots. For instance, `V55RevertFreeEvCap.t.sol` accurately sets `EV_CAP_PACKED_SLOT = 40` and `LOOTBOX_RNG_PACKED_SLOT = 34`. `AfKingConcurrency.t.sol` correctly points `SUBSCRIBERS_SLOT` to 56 and `SUBOF_SLOT` to 53/54, mapping flawlessly to the new `DegenerusGameStorage` tail layout.

**(STORAGE-07) `capBucketCounts` exactness**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** The imprecision is fully defended by its documented downstream clamps, guaranteeing no bounds violations.

**(FC-389-03) `DecClaimRound.totalBurn` comment-vs-accumulator framing**
**VERIFIED SOUND/IDENTICAL:** The `DecClaimRound.totalBurn` accumulator indeed stores effective amounts, so its struct comment ("sum of effective amounts") is **correct**. In `DegenerusGameDecimatorModule.sol`'s `_recordDecimatorBurn`, the `effectiveAmount` is calculated via `_decEffectiveAmount`, and the `uint192 delta = newBurn - prevBurn` passed to `_decUpdateSubbucket` is precisely that effective amount. The uint128 bounds assumption remains sound. *(Note: The comment on the player-level `DecEntry.burn` struct member claiming "Total BURNIE burned" is imprecise, as it stores the effective burn rather than the raw token burn, but this presents no structural threat).*
