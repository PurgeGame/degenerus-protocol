### Primary Finding: SOLV-07 — `whalePassSpent` Double-Credit and Solvency Leak
The rework in `DegenerusGameJackpotModule.sol` introduces a double-accounting error for solo bucket winners who receive whale passes:
1. **Double Credit to `futurePrizePool`:** In `_processSoloBucketWinner`, `whalePassCost` is immediately added to `futurePrizePool` (line 1284). However, in the caller `payDailyJackpot`, the final-day `unpaidDailyEth` calculation (`dailyEthBudget - paidDailyEth`, line 447) treats this same value as "unpaid" because `paidDailyEth` only counts the ETH portion. Consequently, the same `whalePassCost` is added to `futurePrizePool` a second time at line 452.
2. **Under-debit from `currentPrizePool`:** On non-final days, `currentPrizePool` is only decreased by `paidDailyEth` (the ETH share, line 455). The `whalePassCost` portion of the budget remains in `currentPrizePool` while simultaneously being added to `futurePrizePool` in the winner resolution.

This results in the protocol's pool obligations exceeding its actual balance (inflation of phantom ETH), leading to protocol insolvency over time.
