**Methodology (apply to every consumer):**
1. Find every call site that reads rngWordByDay, lootboxRngWordByIndex, lastLootboxRngWord, or any derivative (keccak of word, word >> shift, word & mask, LCG seeded from word)
2. For each read site, identify WHAT DATA gets resolved by that word (e.g., ticket holders, bet amounts, trait selections)
3. Trace that data backward to its commitment point — when was it locked in and by whom?
4. Verify the VRF word was NOT on-chain at the commitment point
5. Look for seam bugs: data that reaches the RNG consumer without passing through the VRF request flow
