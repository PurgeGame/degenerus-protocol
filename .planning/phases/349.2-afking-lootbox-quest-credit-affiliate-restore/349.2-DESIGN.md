## 1. The regression (what + why)

The v55 box redesign (349/349.1) made afking **LOOTBOX-mode** subs *stamp-only* (`GameAfkingModule.sol:735-749`) to kill the ~120k cold box-ledger. Pre-v55, the afking buy went `_autoBuy → GAME.batchPurchase` → the **same Game mint machinery a manual lootbox buy uses**, which calls `quests.handlePurchase` **and** the lootbox `affiliate.payAffiliate`. The stamp-only rewrite dropped both for lootbox subs.

**Smoking gun = asymmetry.** TICKET-mode subs still route through `purchaseWith` (`GameAfkingModule.sol:713-731`) so they KEEP quest-credit + affiliate; only LOOTBOX-mode subs lost them. Nobody decided lootbox afking buyers should stop accruing mint-streak or stop generating protocol affiliate revenue — it fell out of the optimization. **Behavioral + economic regression**; log it in the v55 TERMINAL (352) findings.

## 2. Locked fix (per-buy — USER-chosen)

In the LOOTBOX branch of the process STAGE (`GameAfkingModule.sol:735-749`), at the buy/stamp moment, replicate the **BURNIE side-effects of `purchaseWith`'s lootbox leg** (no cold ledger), mirroring a manual lootbox buy:

1. **Quest** — `quests.handlePurchase(player, ethMintSpendWei = spend, burnieMintUnits = 0, lootBoxAmount = spend, priceWei, PriceLookupLib.priceForLevel(level+1))` (the shape `MintModule.sol:1222` uses for a pure-lootbox buy). Run it **BEFORE** computing the stamped score ("handlers before score", `MintModule.sol:1211`).
2. **Score source change** — capture the streak `handlePurchase` returns and feed it to `_playerActivityScore` for `scorePlus1` (replacing the current pre-action `_questStreakOf` read at `:739-746`). So the stamped score reflects the **post-buy** streak, identical to a manual buy. Still frozen at stamp (FREEZE-03 intact).
3. **Affiliate** — mirror BOTH `purchaseWith` branches (`MintModule.sol:1269` fresh + `:1279` claimable): `affiliate.payAffiliate(_ethToBurnieValue(freshPortion, priceWei), code, player, level+1, isFresh=true, score)` on the fresh-ETH portion (the `ethValue` debited from `afkingFunding`), and the `isFresh=false, score=0` call on the claimable-used portion. The afking `_resolveBuy` already produces the fresh-vs-claimable split.
4. **BURNIE flip-credit (the only "payment")** — accumulate the returned credits (the `questReward` on completion, `MintModule.sol:1232` + both `payAffiliate` kickbacks `:1269/:1279`) into a local and `coinflip.creditFlip(player, total)` once (mirror `MintModule.sol:1366-1367`). `payAffiliate` internally credits the affiliate's BURNIE share to the winner. **All BURNIE — no ETH moves.**

**KEEP unchanged:** the warm Sub stamp (`scorePlus1`, `amount`) — **no cold box ledger** (`lootboxEth`/`lootboxPurchasePacked`/`enqueueBoxForAutoOpen`/`boxPlayers.push` stay gone — the GAS-01 win); the **ETH/`claimablePool` accounting** (`claimablePool -= ethValue` at `:710` + the freed-ETH→box-spend flow — see §3); the **EV-cap at OPEN** (EVCAP-01 — do NOT add `purchaseWith`'s buy-time EV-cap tally at `:1288+`); the **TICKET branch** (`:713-731`); the open leg / `resolveAfkingBox`.

## 3. No ETH / no solvency surface — affiliate + quest rewards are BURNIE flip-credit

**Correction (USER 2026-05-31): ETH has nothing to do with affiliate payments.** `affiliate.payAffiliate` and `quests.handlePurchase` produce **BURNIE flip-credit**, not a cut of the ETH. The ETH spend is only the *valuation basis*: `_ethToBurnieValue(wei, priceWei) = wei * PRICE_COIN_UNIT / priceWei` (`MintModule.sol:1669-1675`) converts it to a BURNIE amount, and `_routeAffiliateReward(player, amount)` is just `coinflip.creditFlip(player, amount)` (`DegenerusAffiliate.sol:745-751`). The affiliate's share, the player's kickback, and the quest reward are all credited as BURNIE via `coinflip.creditFlip`.

**Therefore the existing ETH/`claimablePool` accounting in the lootbox branch is UNCHANGED** — `claimablePool -= ethValue` (`:710`) and the freed-ETH→box-spend flow stay byte-identical. The fix is **purely additive** (BURNIE-credit + quest-state side-effects). **No `claimablePool` reconciliation, no SOLVENCY-01 surface** is introduced by this phase. The real IMPL work is faithful replication of `purchaseWith`'s lootbox-leg BURNIE flip-credit accumulation (§2.4) + the quest-state update + the BURNIE-denominated affiliate routing — none of it touches ETH.

## 5. DEFERRED — weekly affiliate aggregation (NOT in this phase)

USER is open to once-per-week aggregated affiliate IFF non-gameable. It is **not** non-gameable as-is: `payAffiliate` distribution is a winner-takes-all roll seeded by `currentDayIndex()` (`:540-560`) + a non-linear lootbox taper (`:502`) + a per-buy rate by `(isFreshEth, level)` (`:486-495`). Batching collapses N daily rolls → 1 (variance change + lets the settlement day be chosen to land a predictable roll, since the PRNG is known). Only non-gameable if afking adopts a **DETERMINISTIC split** (drop the per-buy roll) — an economic-design change. **Backlog SEED**, not this phase. (Quests are non-linear → per-buy regardless.) Note this is independent of the ETH-vs-BURNIE point: the gameability is in the roll/taper/rate, all BURNIE-denominated.
