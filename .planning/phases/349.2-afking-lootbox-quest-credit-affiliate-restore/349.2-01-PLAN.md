<objective>
Close the v55 box-redesign REGRESSION: afking LOOTBOX-mode subs were rewritten
stamp-only (GameAfkingModule.sol:735-749) to kill the ~120k cold box-ledger and
SILENTLY DROPPED the `quests.handlePurchase` + `affiliate.payAffiliate` that the
pre-v55 `batchPurchase` path gave those buys. (TICKET-mode subs route through
`purchaseWith` at :713-731 and KEPT them — the asymmetry proves it unintended.)

This plan restores them **per-buy** by replicating the BURNIE side-effects of
`purchaseWith`'s lootbox leg in the lootbox stamp branch, MINUS the cold box-ledger
(the stamp replaces it — GAS-01 preserved) and MINUS the buy-time EV-cap tally
(stays AT OPEN — EVCAP-01). The fix is **small + purely additive** (DESIGN §2): at
the buy/stamp moment —

1. **Quest (before score).** Call `quests.handlePurchase(player, ethMintSpendWei =
   amount, burnieMintQty = 0, lootBoxAmount = amount, mintPrice = mp, levelQuestPrice
   = PriceLookupLib.priceForLevel(currentLevel + 1))` — the pure-lootbox shape
   MintModule.sol:1222 uses — BEFORE computing `scorePlus1` ("handlers before score",
   MintModule.sol:1211). For a lootbox sub `amount == cost` (boons OFF), so the spend
   is the full `amount`.
2. **Score source change.** Feed the streak `handlePurchase` RETURNS into
   `_playerActivityScore` for `scorePlus1`, replacing the current pre-action
   `_questStreakOf(player)` read at :739-746. The stamped score then reflects the
   POST-buy streak, identical to a manual buy. Keep the explicit `currentLevel + 1`
   streakBaseLevel (this is the box's open level). Still frozen at stamp (FREEZE-03).
   Apply the recordMintQuestStreak-on-completion path (MintModule.sol:1233-1235).
3. **Affiliate (both branches).** Mirror BOTH `purchaseWith` calls
   (MintModule.sol:1269 fresh + :1279 claimable). The afking fresh-vs-claimable split
   comes straight from `_resolveBuy`: the fresh-ETH portion is `ethValue` (debited
   from afkingFunding), the claimable-used portion is `amount - ethValue`. Call
   `affiliate.payAffiliate(_ethToBurnie(ethValue), bytes32("DGNRS"), player,
   currentLevel + 1, true, uint16(activityScore))` on the fresh portion (when nonzero)
   and the `isFreshEth = false, lootboxActivityScore = 0` variant on the claimable
   portion (when nonzero).
4. **BURNIE flip-credit (the ONLY "payment").** Accumulate the returned credits — the
   `questReward` on completion (MintModule.sol:1232) + both `payAffiliate` kickbacks
   (:1269/:1279) — into ONE local and `coinflip.creditFlip(player, total)` ONCE
   (mirror MintModule.sol:1366-1367). All BURNIE — no ETH moves.

**Affiliate + quest rewards are BURNIE flip-credit, NOT an ETH cut (DESIGN §3).**
`_ethToBurnieValue(wei, priceWei) = wei * PRICE_COIN_UNIT / priceWei` is only the
valuation basis; `_routeAffiliateReward` (DegenerusAffiliate.sol:745-751) is just
`coinflip.creditFlip`. The existing `afkingFunding[src] -= ethValue` +
`claimablePool -= uint128(ethValue)` debit at :708-711 and the freed-ETH → box-spend
flow stay **byte-identical**. **No claimablePool reconciliation, no solvency surface.**

KEEP unchanged: no cold box-ledger (no lootboxEth / lootboxPurchasePacked /
enqueueBoxForAutoOpen / boxPlayers.push — GAS-01); the EV-cap AT OPEN (do NOT add the
MintModule.sol:1288+ buy-time tally — EVCAP-01); the TICKET branch (:713-731); the
open leg / resolveAfkingBox. Weekly affiliate aggregation is DEFERRED (DESIGN §5 —
not non-gameable without a deterministic-split economic change).

Purpose: restore the quest-streak accrual + protocol affiliate revenue that lootbox
afking buyers used to get (and that ticket afking buyers still get), closing the
asymmetry — with zero ETH/solvency change.
Output: ONE batched `contracts/*.sol` diff (GameAfkingModule.sol only), applied +
contracts-only forge-build-clean + DegenerusGame < 24,576, HELD at the
contract-commit gate.
