I have completed the audit of the **BURNIE / coinflip-seeded-emission rework** at commit `a8b702a7`. The investigation focused on verifying the protocol's core invariants regarding BURNIE emission, sDGNRS backing, and storage efficiency.

### Summary of Findings

#### 1. (PRIME-01 — BURNIE-04) sDGNRS Auto-Rebuy Carry Stranded
**FINDING:** The `autoRebuyCarry` accumulated by the sDGNRS contract is invisible to its redemption backing.
- **Root Cause:** `StakedDegenerusStonk.sol` calculates `burnieOwed` using `previewClaimCoinflips`, which only considers settled `claimableStored` and unresolved winning days. It does **not** read the `autoRebuyCarry`.
- **Impact:** Since sDGNRS is auto-claimed every resolved day (keeping its `lastClaim` current), the `autoRebuyCarry` is a "black hole" for value. Redeemers are progressively **under-credited** as sDGNRS's rolling coinflip position grows, and the contract lacks a mechanism to claim or liquidate this carry.

#### 2. (PRIME-02 — BURNIE-05) VAULT Seed Window-Aging Forfeiture
**FINDING:** The VAULT's 4M BURNIE seed emission is at risk of silent forfeiture.
- **Root Cause:** The `BurnieCoinflip._claimCoinflipsInternal` logic enforces a 30-day claim window for players with `lastClaim == 0`.
- **Impact:** Unlike sDGNRS, the VAULT is not auto-claimed. If the operator fails to call `coinClaimCoinflips` within the first 30 days of the game, the entire Day 1-20 seed window (~2M BURNIE expected) is **unrecoverably forfeited**.

*   **(BURNIE-01) Survive-before-mint:** **VERIFIED SOUND.** Every BURNIE source (Seeds, Degenerette, Afking, Lootbox, Redemptions) gates on a survived coinflip before minting.
*   **(BURNIE-02) Seed Emission Conservation:** **VERIFIED SOUND.** The 8M total stake (~4M EV) accurately replaces the removed 2M+2M fixed lumps.
*   **(BURNIE-03) Auto-Rebuy Latch Monotonicity:** **VERIFIED SOUND.** The `sdgnrsAutoRebuyArmed` latch is monotone and correctly handles the transition from wallet-mints to carry-rolling.
