Based on the audit of the **Degenerus Protocol** at frozen commit `a8b702a7`, focusing on the **PERMISSIONLESS-COMPOSITION** slice (ACCESS-01..05), the findings are as follows:

### 1. (ACCESS-01) Every permissionless claim credits ONLY the beneficiary.
**VERIFIED SOUND:**
*   **Decimator Jackpot:** `claimDecimatorJackpot(address player, uint24 lvl)` and its `Many` batch variant credit `player`’s claimable winnings and award lootboxes solely to `player` via `_claimDecimatorJackpotFor`.
*   **Redemption Claim:** `claimRedemption(address player, uint24 day)` and its `Many` batch variant forward value to `player` (via `creditRedemptionDirect` and `resolveRedemptionLootbox`) or to `sDGNRS`’s own claimable (for dust forfeits), never to `msg.sender`. 
*   **Coinflip Carry:** `claimCoinflipCarry(address player, uint256 amount)` is restricted to `msg.sender` or an approved operator via `_resolvePlayer(player)`, and it mints `BURNIE` tokens directly to `player` (`BurnieCoinflip.sol:787`).
*   **Conclusion:** Third-party triggers hold no exclusive timing edge and cannot extract value from the beneficiary.

### 2. (ACCESS-02) Keeper box-bounties are net-NEGATIVE vs real gas + un-manufacturable.
**VERIFIED SOUND:**
*   **Bounty Economics:** `BOX_BOUNTY_ETH_TARGET` is `15e12` wei ($0.000015$ ETH) for Decimator (`~30k` gas) and `24e12` wei ($0.000024$ ETH) for Redemption (`~48k` gas). 
    *   **At 20 gwei:** Cost for Decimator is `30,000 * 20e-9 = 0.0006` ETH, which is **40x** the reward. 
    *   **At 5 gwei:** Cost is `0.00015` ETH, which is **10x** the reward.
    *   **Liquid Value:** The reward is paid in illiquid `BURNIE` flip-credits (50% flip risk + peg discount), making the realized value `~30%` of the target ETH-value.
*   **Un-manufacturability:** Each settle-able entry requires a real `BURNIE` or `sDGNRS` burn ($1$ whole token floor for redemptions). A keeper cannot fabricate work; they can only crank work generated by real players.
*   **Issuance Bound:** `BURNIE` dilution is bounded by real burn activity and capped by daily supply limits (50% for redemptions).

### 3. (ACCESS-03) Forced claim-timing on the winner is INERT.
**VERIFIED SOUND:**
*   **Divergence:** While adjacent levels can have significant ticket price jumps (e.g., $0.16$ ETH at Level 99 to $0.24$ ETH at Level 100), these are "milestone" levels with corresponding jumps in jackpot rewards. 
*   **Magnitude:** The forced timing (typically resolving a claim earlier than the winner might have) is generally **beneficial** or neutral for the winner, as it provides tickets for closer levels (higher probability of winning sooner). The frozen seed ensures the outcome (win/loss) is immune to timing steering.

### 4. (ACCESS-04) Partial-balance redemption-leg solvency holds under same-block bursts.
**VERIFIED SOUND:**
*   **Accounting:** `_pendingRedemptionEthValue` is lowered by the exact `totalRolledEth` per claim (`StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:849`). 
*   **Funding Mix:** Each leg (`ethDirect`, `lootboxEth`, `forfeitEth`) uses `min(balance, amount)` ETH and pulls the remainder as `stETH`. 
*   **Reservation:** The `MAX_ROLL` (175%) reservation made at submission/resolution covers any possible roll (25-175%). The `stETH-remainder` pull ensures that even if ETH is drained by earlier claims in the same block, the total value (ETH + stETH) always matches the roll. No ETH is stranded as all pending liability is backed by the contract's combined reserves.

### 5. (ACCESS-05) Gates and Reentrancy are intact.
**VERIFIED SOUND:**
*   **Gates:** `prizePoolFrozen` correctly blocks Decimator/Redemption claims during the RNG window. `rngLocked` blocks `claimCoinflipCarry`. Post-gameOver redemptions are strictly self-claim only (`player == msg.sender`).
*   **Reentrancy:**
    *   **CEI:** All entrypoints delete the pending claim slot and debit the ledger **before** the untrusted `.call`.
    *   **Ordering:** In `StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:1111` and `DegenerusGame.sol:1905`, `stETH` is transferred **before** the untrusted ETH `.call`. This ensures that a reentrant call (e.g., to `distributeYieldSurplus` or `burn()`) sees the correct remaining reserves and no "in-flight" assets are double-counted as free backing.
    *   **Callee Gates:** `resolveRedemptionLootbox` and `creditRedemptionDirect` correctly gate `msg.sender == SDGNRS`, preventing external bypass.

### 6. (FC-393-04) Widened claim-loop gas worst-case.
**VERIFIED SOUND:**
*   **Efficiency:** The packed storage (32 days/slot for results, 2 days/slot for stakes) ensures that long walks (365/1460 days) consume minimal gas due to EVM SLOAD caching.
*   **Liveness:** The `sDGNRS` auto-settle in `advanceGame` is kept current on every call, reducing the loop to $O(1)$ in normal operation. Cold-SLOAD walks are caller-paid and isolated from the `advanceGame` liveness chain.

**Conclusion:** All protocol beliefs for this slice are confirmed as robust and correctly implemented at commit `a8b702a7`.
