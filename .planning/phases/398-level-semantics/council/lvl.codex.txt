**Findings**

1. [contracts/DegenerusQuests.sol](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/DegenerusQuests.sol:1979): level quest eligibility is keyed to `lvl + 1`, even though the quest is rolled for the current level.
Expected: after [AdvanceModule rolls the level quest for the jackpot phase](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/modules/DegenerusGameAdvanceModule.sol:536), eligibility should require mint units at current `lvl`.
Actual: `_isLevelQuestEligible` requires `unitsLvl == lvl + 1` at [DegenerusQuests.sol:1983](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/DegenerusQuests.sol:1983), and completion emits `lvl + 1` at [DegenerusQuests.sol:2089](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/DegenerusQuests.sol:2089).
Effect: a player who bought the required 4+ units into the level being resolved can fail the current level quest gate; the quest instead recognizes next-level mint units.

2. [contracts/modules/DegenerusGameWhaleModule.sol](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/modules/DegenerusGameWhaleModule.sol:177): whale/pass ticket benefits always start at `level + 1`.
Expected under the review rule: whale ticket buys should target `_activeTicketLevel()`, meaning current `level` during jackpot phase.
Actual: whale bundle uses `passLevel = level + 1` and queues from there at [GameWhaleModule.sol:209](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/modules/DegenerusGameWhaleModule.sol:209), lazy pass uses `currentLevel + 1` at [GameWhaleModule.sol:439](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/modules/DegenerusGameWhaleModule.sol:439), and deity affiliate whale-pass tickets start from `level + 1` at [GameWhaleModule.sol:588](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/modules/DegenerusGameWhaleModule.sol:588).
Effect: during jackpot phase, these buys do not create current-level tickets; their pass streak/front-load also begins one level later. If whale/pass products are intended to be future-only, this is a documented exception rather than a bug, but it does not match the stated rule.
