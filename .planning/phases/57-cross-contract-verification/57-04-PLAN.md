**1. Prize Pool Distribution Intent**
- How are jackpot pools split between buckets? Is the 4-bucket trait-matched distribution the intended game-theoretic design?

**2. Whale Economics Intent**
- Is the deity pass pricing (24 + T(n) ETH with triangular numbers) the intended barrier-to-entry curve?
- Is the deity refund (flat 20 ETH, FIFO, budget-capped) the intended risk-reward for deity pass holders?
- Is the whale bundle's 2.4 ETH / 4 ETH tier split the intended whale pricing?

**3. Game Lifecycle Intent**
- Is the x00-level BAF+Decimator overlap (max 50% draw) the intended behavior?
- Is the 3-day emergency stall gate the intended admin power limit?
