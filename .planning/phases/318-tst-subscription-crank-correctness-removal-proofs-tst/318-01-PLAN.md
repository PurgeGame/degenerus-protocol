<objective>
Repair the Foundry deploy fixture so `DeployProtocol.setUp()` stops reverting. This is the linchpin of Phase 318 — every other plan in this phase depends on it (Wave 1, dependency-zero).

Phase 317 wired the SUB-09 self-subscribe: `DegenerusVault` (:473) and `StakedDegenerusStonk` (:379) call `afKing.subscribe(address(this), ...)` on `IAfKingSubscribe(ContractAddresses.AF_KING)` at deploy time. `AF_KING` is currently `address(0)`, so those constructor calls hit a non-contract and `setUp()` reverts with "call to non-contract 0x0" for ~41 suites (197 of 533 tests runnable at HEAD per 317-08). The fix is to deploy a live `contracts/AfKing.sol` keeper into the fixture and pin `AF_KING` to its predicted address.

Purpose: Unblock ~336 tests so the SAFE-01..04 / JGAS-03 coverage plans (Waves 2+) can reach their bodies, and EMPIRICALLY validate the 317-08 slot re-derivation (currently proven-by-derivation only).

Output: An AfKing-aware deploy fixture; a passing DeployCanary; the previously-reverting VRF/lootbox suites reaching their bodies green with no slot drift.

SCOPE GUARD: This plan touches ONLY test-harness files + the deploy scripts + `ContractAddresses.sol` (freely modifiable per the project's ContractAddresses policy). It MUST NOT modify any other `contracts/*.sol` — the rest of the contract tree is committed and frozen for this phase. If you believe a non-ContractAddresses production change is required, STOP and escalate; that is not a test plan.
