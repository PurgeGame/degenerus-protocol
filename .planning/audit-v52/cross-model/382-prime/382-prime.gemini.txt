The v61 new contract surface (**PRIME-01..04**) was audited at commit `c4d48008`. The system is remarkably robust across the five requested areas, with specific findings being confirmed as intended design trade-offs rather than vulnerabilities.

### Findings Summary

#### Area 1: afking-as-payment spine (PRIME-02) — CLEAN
**Property:** Solvency identity `claimablePool == Σ (claimableWinnings + afkingFunding)` and slot-packing integrity.
- **Slot Packing:** `balancesPacked[player]` (Slot 7) is packed as `[afking:hi128 | claimable:lo128]`.
  - **Carry/Collision:** A 127→128 carry from `claimable` to `afking` is physically impossible for ETH (max supply ~1.2e26 wei vs 128-bit cap ~3.4e38). Hi-half overflows wrap the 256-bit word without affecting the low half.
  - **Borrow/Underflow:** `_debitClaimable` (L913) explicitly guards against low-half borrows. `_debitAfking` (L928) uses a full-word subtraction that reverts on underflow (hi128 < amount), preventing silent borrows.
- **Solvency:** The identity is strictly maintained. Every debit from `claimable` or `afking` (e.g., `_deliverAfkingBuy`, `_settleShortfall`, `withdrawAfkingFunding`, `claimWinnings`) is paired with an identical `claimablePool` debit. Every credit (`_creditAfkingValue`, `_creditBoxProceeds`) is paired with a `claimablePool` credit.
- **State Var:** `DegenerusGameStorage.balancesPacked` (L418), `DegenerusGameStorage.claimablePool` (L365).

#### Area 2: combo funding-mix parity tweak (PRIME-02) — CLEAN
**Property:** Parity of `mintCost` with canonical price path and fund-flow integrity.
- **Parity:** `buyLootboxAndPresaleBox` recomputes `mintCost` using `PriceLookupLib.priceForLevel(jackpotPhaseFlag ? level : level + 1)`, which exactly matches the logic in `_purchaseForWith`.
- **Fund Flow:** The `msg.value` split correctly prioritizes the mint leg and passes the remainder to the box leg.
- **No Stranding:** Excess `msg.value` (refund) from the presale-box cap crossing is correctly credited to the player's `afking` balance at `DegenerusGameMintModule.sol:1615`.
- **State Var:** `DegenerusGameMintModule.buyLootboxAndPresaleBox` (L1501).

#### Area 3: cashout-curse spine (PRIME-03) — CLEAN
**Property:** State machine integrity and griefing resistance.
- **Logic:** The SET (on stale claim >5 days) and CURE (on ≥1 ticket buy) logic is sound. The cure happens BEFORE the score calculation in the same transaction (`DegenerusGameMintModule.sol:1348-1353`), providing intended UX where the curing buy is not penalized.
- **Observation:** New users (history=0) are "stale" and receive a curse on their first claim if the game is past day 5. However, since new players have 0 activity bonus, the -200bps penalty has zero impact, and the curse is cleared by their first buy.
- **State Var:** `GameAfkingModule.maybeCurse` (L1668).

#### Area 4: deity-smite spine (PRIME-04) — CLEAN
**Property:** Agreement on "active afker" status between immunity and eviction.
- **Immunity:** Smite and cashout-curse immunity for active afkers (`_subOf[player].dailyQuantity != 0`) is consistent.
- **Discrepancy:** A player with an expired pass stays in the subscriber set (and immune) until they are reached by the advance cursor and evicted. This is an intended trade-off of the chunked, gas-bounded eviction model.
- **State Var:** `GameAfkingModule.smite` (L1710), `GameAfkingModule.processSubscriberStage` (L1214).

#### Area 5: Carried candidate FC4 (Affiliate Freeze) — CLEAN
**Property:** Atomicity of `drainAffiliateBase` and cancellation robustness.
- **Atomic Drain:** `drainAffiliateBase` correctly zeroes the slot to prevent double-spending. `DegenerusAffiliate.claim` handles duplicate entries safely.
- **Unsubscribe:** `subscribe(cancel)` correctly triggers a claim and clears both `pendingBurnie` and `affiliateBase`.
- **Frozen Accounts:** No state in the affiliate or coinflip contracts blocks the auto-claim on cancellation for "frozen" accounts (Whale Passes). Forfeiture of rewards upon forced eviction is documented design.
- **State Var:** `GameAfkingModule.subscribe` (L341), `DegenerusAffiliate.claim` (L641).
