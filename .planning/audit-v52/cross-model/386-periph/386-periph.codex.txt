**Finding**
PROPERTY: a completed redemption’s direct stETH leg must not be counted as backing for a new burn before it is transferred out.

CALL SEQUENCE: attacker keeps some sDGNRS after submitting a prior redemption, waits until that day is resolved/unlocked, then calls `claimRedemption(day)` when sDGNRS has `0 < ETH balance < ethDirect` plus stETH. `claimRedemption` releases `pendingRedemptionEthValue` and deletes the claim before payout (`contracts/StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:726-731`). `_payEth` then sends the partial ETH to the attacker before transferring the stETH remainder (`contracts/StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:951-957`). In the ETH receive hook, the attacker calls `burn()`/`burnWrapped()`; once RNG is unlocked, those paths are allowed (`contracts/StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:561-589`). The reentrant burn computes backing from live ETH + live stETH - pending (`contracts/StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:866-870`), so it includes stETH already owed to the old claim. It then reserves a new redemption via `pullRedemptionReserve` and increments pending (`contracts/StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:908-911`), while the Game stETH-reserve fallback only checks the live sDGNRS stETH balance and does not move it (`contracts/DegenerusGame.sol:2091-2097`). The original claim finally transfers that stETH out, leaving the new redemption over-reserved/underbacked.

SEVERITY: HIGH. This breaks the redemption reserve identity and can double-count value during the mixed ETH/stETH direct payout branch, diluting or stranding later redeemers.

Why protections do not stop it: same-claim double-claim is blocked by the delete, but the delete plus pending decrement is exactly what makes the owed stETH visible as free backing during the callback. The RNG/liveness guards do not apply after the resolving advance has unlocked RNG. The C-2 stETH approval fix holds for the lootbox leg (`contracts/StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:389-392`, `contracts/DegenerusGame.sol:2021-2025`), but it does not protect the direct `_payEth` branch. Fix direction: send stETH before the untrusted ETH call, keep the pending reserve until all payout legs complete, or add a reentrancy guard over redemption claim/burn paths.
