--- AREA A: sDGNRS redemption backing completeness (BURNIE-04) ---
**VERDICT: DEFECT**

Mechanism / structural fact:
The `burnieOwed` calculation in `StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:1031` relies on `coinflip.previewClaimCoinflips(sDGNRS)`, which consists of `_viewClaimableCoin + claimableStored` (`BurnieCoinflip.sol:971-975`). However, `sDGNRS` operates on perpetual auto-rebuy with zero take-profit (`BurnieCoinflip.sol:877-892`). In this mode, `_claimCoinflipsInternal` (called daily for sDGNRS) moves all winnings into `autoRebuyCarry` and never increments `claimableStored` (`BurnieCoinflip.sol:487-495`).
ACTIONABLE: Because `previewClaimCoinflips` does not read `autoRebuyCarry`, the redemption backing for sDGNRS is severely under-reported. Winnings accrued post-day-20 are stranded in the `autoRebuyCarry` of the sDGNRS account and can neither be withdrawn to cover redemptions (as `_claimCoinflipsAmount` also ignores carry, `BurnieCoinflip.sol:339/364`) nor reflected in the share price. While conservative (prevents over-credit), it causes a backing-completeness gap and permanent stranding of protocol assets.

--- AREA B: VAULT coinflip seed window-aging (BURNIE-05) ---
**VERDICT: DEFECT**

Mechanism / structural fact:
The `BurnieCoinflip` constructor seeds days 1-20 to the VAULT (`BurnieCoinflip.sol:189-192`). The claim window logic in `_viewClaimableCoin` (and `_claimCoinflipsInternal`) enforces a `minClaimableDay = latestDay - windowDays` clamp (`BurnieCoinflip.sol:1008-1014`). For a player's first claim (`startDay == 0`), `windowDays` is `COIN_CLAIM_FIRST_DAYS = 30` (`BurnieCoinflip.sol:137/1005`).
ACTIONABLE: If the VAULT owner performs the first claim at `flipsClaimableDay >= 51`, the `minClaimableDay` will be `>= 21`, and the loop `cursor = startDay + 1` will start at `>= 22`, silently skipping the seed days 1-20. This is a self-inflicted operational risk where the protocol-owned VAULT forfeits its initial BURNIE allocation due to simple liveness/operational delay.

--- AREA C: keeper box-bounty economics vs real gas (ACCESS-02) ---
**VERDICT: SOUND**

Mechanism / structural fact:
The bounties (15e12 and 24e12 wei) are hardcoded to reimburse gas at a ~0.5-gwei reference (`DegenerusGameDecimatorModule.sol:117`, `StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:343`). At real-world gas prices (e.g., 20-50 gwei), the bounty is ~40x-100x smaller than the gas cost. Furthermore, being paid as an illiquid `creditFlip` (subject to a ~50% win rate and a ~0.59 peg) further reduces the realized EV to <1% of gas.
The area is sound because there is no "positive-EV faucet": each box requires a real on-chain burn (decimator) or 175%-reserved ETH submit (redemption) to create. Settle-able entries cannot be Sybil-manufactured; they are liveness work performed at a loss by keepers or self-settled by players.

--- AREA D: partial-balance redemption-leg burst solvency (ACCESS-04) ---
**VERDICT: SOUND**

Mechanism / structural fact:
The conservation identity $\sum(\text{legs}) == \sum(\text{rolled}) == \sum(\text{released})$ holds perfectly. `_claimRedemptionFor` calculates the `totalRolledEth` and splits it into `ethDirect`, `lootboxEth`, and `forfeitEth` (`StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:833-853`). It then releases exactly `totalRolledEth` from `_pendingRedemptionEthValue` (`:856`).
Each leg re-reads `address(this).balance` (`:903, :910, :919`) and forwards `min(bal, legAmount)` as `msg.value`. The `DegenerusGameLootboxModule` (via `delegatecall` from `GAME`) pulls the remainder `amount - msg.value` as `stETH` via `transferFrom` (`DegenerusGameLootboxModule.sol:934/1011`). Since the 175% MAX reservation was physically segregated at submit into the sDGNRS balance (as pure ETH or pure stETH), the contract is guaranteed to have the total required value in its combined (ETH + stETH) balance, preventing strands.
