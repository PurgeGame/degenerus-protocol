All four safety claims made by the previous reviewer are confirmed to hold based on a detailed re-examination of the frozen source code at commit `a8b702a7`.

### CANDIDATE 1: reward money-pump (ECON-04)
**Verdict: HOLDS**
**Mechanism / structural fact:** The protocol implements multiple structural barriers that prevent a closed positive-EV loop. 
1. **Recycle Gating:** The 10% mint-recycle kicker (`DegenerusGameMintModule.sol:1740-1747`) is only awarded for spending *claimable winnings* (won value), not `msg.value`. This prevents a simple ETH-in loop.
2. **Illiquidity:** The kicker is awarded as `creditFlip` (illiquid BURNIE stake) which must survive a 50/50 flip in `BurnieCoinflip.sol`, reducing its realized value by at least 50% before any peg discount.
3. **EV Caps:** The "Activity Score" EV uplift for lootboxes is strictly capped at a 10 ETH spend per player, per level (`DegenerusGameStorage.sol:1549-1550` and `DegenerusGameLootboxModule.sol:474-510`). Any spend beyond 10 ETH reverts to neutral (100%) EV, regardless of the player's score.
4. **Recursion Limit:** Recursive money-pumping via the ETH-spin is blocked by the `allowEthSpin=false` flag on all recirculated boxes (`DegenerusGameLootboxModule.sol:1955-2041`), which replaces ETH rewards with future tickets.

### CANDIDATE 2: quest-streak pump (ECON-06)
**Verdict: HOLDS**
**Mechanism / structural fact:** The quest system is robustly protected against double-counting and ceiling escalation.
1. **Deduplication:** Every quest slot completion is deduped per-day via the `completionMask` (`DegenerusQuests.sol:1711`). Once a bit is set, any further action for that slot on that day returns early.
2. **Mutual Exclusion:** The `_effectiveQuestStreak` function (`DegenerusGameStorage.sol:2284-2293`) reads either the manual streak *or* the afking compute-on-read streak, never both. 
3. **Streak Neutrality:** While afking, manual slot-0 completions are explicitly made streak-neutral (`DegenerusQuests.sol:1745-1755`), preventing the "double-channel" where a player might get credit for both the funded auto-mint and a manual mint on the same day.
4. **Fixed Ceilings:** All reward-scaling logic uses fixed bps caps (e.g., 30,500 ROI cap in `DegenerusGameDegeneretteModule.sol:194` and 100-streak factor clamp in `DegenerusGameDecimatorModule.sol:956`) that are significantly lower than the `uint16` or `uint24` storage limits.

### CANDIDATE 3: whalePassCost double-credit (SOLV-07)
**Verdict: HOLDS**
**Mechanism / structural fact:** The accounting in `DegenerusGameJackpotModule.sol` is perfectly balanced across all payout paths.
1. **Summed Liability:** In `_handleSoloBucketWinner` (`L1218-1222`), both `paid` (ETH) and `wpSpent` (Whale Pass cost) are added to `paidDelta`. This ensures `paidDailyEth` accurately reflects the total value removed from the prize pool.
2. **Balanced Flow:** On non-final days, `currentPrizePool` is reduced by `paidDailyEth` (`L455`). Simultaneously, `claimablePool` is increased by the ETH portion and `futurePrizePool` is increased by the `wpSpent` portion (`_processSoloBucketWinner:1274`). The net change to the protocol's total obligation is zero.
3. **Final Day Overflow:** On final days, any "unpaid" budget (`dailyEthBudget - paidDailyEth`) is correctly routed to `futurePrizePool` (`L452`). Since `paidDailyEth` correctly includes the `whalePassCost`, that cost is never part of the "unpaid" overflow, preventing any double-credit to the future pool.

### CANDIDATE 4: cross-round decimator seed collision (RNG-04)
**Verdict: HOLDS**
**Mechanism / structural fact:** The truncation of the VRF word to `uint32` (`DegenerusGameDecimatorModule.sol:277`) is benign due to the lack of player influence and independent reward scaling.
1. **Post-Commitment Randomness:** The VRF word is requested and revealed only after the level ends and player burn commitments are frozen. A player cannot see or influence the `uint32` word before committing their tokens.
2. **Independent Scaling:** The lootbox reward magnitude is scaled by the `amount` of the specific claim (`DegenerusGameLootboxModule.sol:1210`), which varies per player/level. Even if the seeds collide (same `rngWord` and same `player`), the actual value received is tied to the independent burn amount.
3. **No Steering:** There are no player-controllable inputs (like timestamp or previous block hash) that enter the lootbox seed calculation in `resolveLootboxDirect` (`DegenerusGameLootboxModule.sol:884`), ensuring the collision probability remains at the theoretical $1/2^{32}$ floor.
