## The unit convention (ground truth, verified 3 ways)

`ticketsOwedPacked[level][buyer]` packs `[uint32 owed][uint8 remainder]`; `owed` is denominated in **ENTRIES**. **1 whole ticket = 4 entries; 1 entry = `priceForLevel(level)/4`.** Proof: (a) a 1-whole-ticket purchase costs `priceWei·ticketQuantity/(4·TICKET_SCALE)` with `ticketQuantity=400` → `_queueTicketsScaled(400)` → `owed += 4`; (b) `_budgetToTicketUnits = (budget<<2)/price = 4·budget/price`; (c) MintModule far-future-swap comment "owed is in entries, 4 per whole ticket". `_queueTickets(q)` adds `q` directly to `owed` → its arg is entries. `TICKET_SCALE = 100`.
