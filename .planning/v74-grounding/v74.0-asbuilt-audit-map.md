## Top attack surfaces (ranked)

1. **[SPINE solvency]** Purchase hot-path solvency fold — per-player claimable/afking debits happen inside `_callTicketPurchase` + the lootbox shortfall settle, but `claimablePool` is decremented once later (~L1564), with a boon-consume delegatecall reentrancy window between. A mismatch on any `payKind` branch, or a reader observing the pool mid-window, mints unbacked credit.
2. **[DOMINANT RNG-freeze]** VRF-death deadman + mid-day RNG recovery fold — premature fire, un-latched mid-drain evaporation, a steerable fallback word, or a stale mid-day `requestId` honored after promotion could brick terminal release or steer a terminal jackpot.
3. **[RNG + HIGH gas]** Queue-gate removal from the 3 shared sinks + fail-open `_swapTicketSlot` + the `didWork`/`drained` same-tx-composition guards — queue-window tickets reaching a manipulable jackpot (RNG) or a false `didWork=false` re-enabling same-tx batch+BAF/jackpot composition (>16.7M game-over).
4. **[DOMINANT RNG + SPINE]** sDGNRS level-start lootbox — a once-per-level box sized at 5% of a LIVE `_claimableOf(SDGNRS)` read in the pre-RNG STAGE, funded from claimable and routed into the box prize pool, bypassing `_resolveBuy`'s normal split. Fresh RNG-freeze seam + new backing-conservation path.
5. **[EV-RTP + solvency]** `payAffiliateCombined` — one entropy roll / one leaderboard RMW / one `handleAffiliate` hop for the whole buy, returning `winnerCredit` to be credited by the out-of-cluster MintModule caller; credit conservation now spans the call boundary, and the collapse from up-to-4 rolls to one changes winner-distribution + floor-of-sum rounding.
6. **[access-trust]** Broad permissionless-settlement relaxation — each relaxed path must settle value only to the owner and source spend only from a consenting party.
7. **[access-trust]** Admin governance timing — 44h sawtooth gate, `vote()` kill-on-recovery reorder, the new unconditional payable `receive()` force-forwarding native to VAULT.
8. **[EV-RTP]** Foil + Degenerette EV byte-equivalence — foil score basis moved to `handleFoilPurchase`'s `streakSnapshot`; Degenerette resolve helpers merged with strict/non-strict batch tolerance + a cross-bet `ResolveAcc` fold.
