<objective>
Revise the GameAfkingModule REQUIRED-PATH process STAGE for the 349.1 redesign,
parts (1) the NO-ORPHAN guard and (2) the P2 ticket/lootbox split, plus dropping
the dead per-day epoch write:

1. **NO-ORPHAN guard (§3 — the load-bearing correctness rule).** A box is STAMPED
   at process (day D) but OPENED later; the box exists ONLY as
   `(Sub stamp + lastAutoBoughtDay)` with no cold ledger. ANY mutation of the Sub
   or removal from `_subscribers` between stamp and open ORPHANS the box (the
   player was debited at stamp, gets nothing). There are FOUR orphan paths —
   re-stamp, cancel-reclaim, pass-eviction, funding-skip-kill. The fix is ONE
   guard at the TOP of the loop: a sub with a pending unopened box
   (`lastOpenedDay < lastAutoBoughtDay`) is left ENTIRELY untouched this cycle and
   re-processed (now boxless) in a LATER cycle, after `_autoOpen` opens it. SKIP,
   not force-open — keeps the heavy open out of the gas-critical advance chain.

2. **Drop the epoch write + epochIndex param.** Storage (plan 01) deleted
   `_afkingEpoch`. The STAGE no longer writes `_afkingEpoch[processDay]` and no
   longer needs `epochIndex` — the box reads the LIVE level + `rngWordByDay[day]`
   at open (plan 03). `processSubscriberStage` signature drops the first param.

3. **P2 ticket/lootbox split (CONFIRMED BUG, §6).** `_resolveBuy` already returns
   `isTicket` and sets `amount = effectiveQty*400` (entry-units) for ticket mode,
   but the stamp+open path always feeds `amount` to the box as a WEI lootbox spend
   → a garbage micro-box, no tickets queued. Route FLAG_USE_TICKETS subs to queue
   whole tickets at the frozen level (the existing ticket-queue mechanic, no box);
   lootbox-mode subs keep the box stamp.

Purpose: the STAGE is the producer of every per-sub box stamp the open consumes
(plan 03). The no-orphan guard is what makes the deferred-open model correct. The
P2 split is what makes ticket subs actually buy tickets.

Output: a revised `processSubscriberStage` (and the `_resolveBuy`-driven body) in
`GameAfkingModule.sol`.

NOTE — REVISION of a HELD diff. `read_first` the actual file; the line anchors are
guidance. The open-leg (`_openAfkingBox`/`_afkingBoxReady`) and `resolveAfkingBox`
rewrites are PLAN 03 — do not touch them here (they will still reference
`_unpackAfkingEpoch` / `lastOpenedIndex` and not compile until 03; that is fine,
this is one batched diff built once at the end in plan 05).
