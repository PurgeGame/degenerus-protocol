### 3. (LEGACY-02b) MINTDIV index alignment
**FINDING:** PROPERTY broken (Lockstep Divergence)
- **RATIONALE:** While the v50 fix added `processed += take` (MintModule:668/930) to track progress within a loop, the `processed` cursor is **NOT persisted** during a "mid-queue stop" caused by write-budget exhaustion. `ticketCursor` persists the player index `idx` (MintModule:703), but `processed` is re-declared as 0 on every call (MintModule:582/880).
- **CALL SEQUENCE:** A player with 1000 tickets is split across multiple `advanceGame` chunks due to the `WRITES_BUDGET_SAFE = 550` limit. Chunk 1 processes `take=199` tickets (a non-multiple of 4) and stops. Chunk 2 resumes for the same player but re-initializes `processed = 0`. 
- **STATE VAR:** `processed` (local) @ `contracts/modules/DegenerusGameMintModule.sol:582`.
- **SEVERITY:** **SPINE** (Value Non-Equivalence).
- **WHY:** The quadrant offset `(uint8(i & 3) << 6)` (MintModule:761) depends on the `processed` index `i`. If `processed` resets to 0 mid-player, the 200th ticket (which should be quadrant 3) is processed as quadrant 0. This causes a **non-deterministic trait distribution** (gas-dependent) and a quadrant bias that players can exploit by manipulating the queue state to force budget splits at specific offsets. Note: `baseKey` correctly includes `owed`, ensuring the PRNG seed itself remains distinct, but the quadrant shift remains a direct break of the intended value-equivalence.
