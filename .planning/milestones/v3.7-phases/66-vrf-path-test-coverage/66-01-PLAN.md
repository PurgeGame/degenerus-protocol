**File: test/fuzz/VRFPathCoverage.t.sol**

1. **Pragma:** `pragma solidity ^0.8.26;`
2. **Imports:** DeployProtocol, MockVRFCoordinator, MintPaymentKind
3. **Contract:** `contract VRFPathCoverage is DeployProtocol`

4. **setUp():** `_deployProtocol();`

5. **Helpers (reuse patterns from VRFStallEdgeCases.t.sol):**
   - `_completeDay(uint256 vrfWord)`: advanceGame -> fulfill -> loop until unlocked (50 iterations max)
   - `_doCoordinatorSwap() returns (MockVRFCoordinator newVRF)`: deploy new MockVRFCoordinator, createSubscription, addConsumer, vm.prank(address(admin)), game.updateVrfCoordinatorAndSub
   - `_stallAndSwap(uint256 gapDays) returns (MockVRFCoordinator newVRF)`: vm.warp(block.timestamp + gapDays * 1 days), then _doCoordinatorSwap
   - `_resumeAfterSwap(MockVRFCoordinator newVRF, uint256 vrfWord)`: advanceGame -> fulfill on newVRF -> loop until unlocked
   - `_makePurchase(address player, uint256 lootboxAmt)`: vm.deal, vm.prank, game.purchase (pattern from LootboxRngLifecycle.t.sol)

6. **Test functions (6 parametric fuzz tests):**

   a. `function test_gapBackfillSingleDay_fuzz(uint256 vrfWord1, uint256 vrfWord2) public`:
      - Bound vrfWord1, vrfWord2 to [1, type(uint256).max]
      - Complete day 1 (vrfWord1)
      - Trigger day 2 VRF request (advanceGame), then stall: vm.warp to absolute day 4 timestamp (4 * 86400)
      - Coordinator swap
      - Resume with vrfWord2 on new coordinator
      - Assert: game.rngWordForDay(2) != 0 (the 1 gap day was backfilled)
      - Assert: game.rngWordForDay(3) != 0 (the gap day was backfilled)
      - Assert: !game.rngLocked()
      - Assert: game.currentDayView() >= 4

   b. `function test_gapBackfillMultiDay_fuzz(uint256 vrfWord1, uint256 vrfWord2, uint8 rawGapDays) public`:
      - Bound rawGapDays to [3, 30]
      - Complete day 1
      - Trigger day 2 VRF, then stall + swap at rawGapDays gap
      - Resume
      - For each gap day d (from day after swap trigger to day before resume), assert game.rngWordForDay(d) != 0
      - Assert all gap day words are unique (compare pairwise or collect into set)
      - Assert !game.rngLocked()

   c. `function test_gapBackfillMaxGap_fuzz(uint256 vrfWord1, uint256 vrfWord2) public`:
      - Complete day 1
      - Trigger day 2 VRF, then stall + swap at 120-day gap (maximum death clock)
      - Measure gas: `uint256 gasBefore = gasleft();` before resume, `uint256 gasUsed = gasBefore - gasleft();` after
      - Assert gasUsed < 25_000_000 (25M gas ceiling from STALL-03)
      - Assert all 120 gap days have nonzero words
      - Assert !game.rngLocked()

   d. `function test_gapBackfillWithMidDayPending_fuzz(uint256 vrfWord1, uint256 vrfWord2, uint256 lootboxAmt) public`:
      - Bound lootboxAmt to [0.01 ether, 2 ether]
      - Complete day 1
      - Make a purchase with lootbox amount on day 2 (triggers lootbox RNG state)
      - Trigger day 2 VRF via advanceGame
      - Call game.requestLootboxRng() (creates mid-day pending state)
      - Stall: warp forward 5 days
      - Coordinator swap (should clear midDayTicketRngPending)
      - Resume with vrfWord2
      - Assert !game.rngLocked()
      - Assert all gap days have nonzero words
      - Record lootboxRngIndex after recovery, assert it matches expected (no orphaned indices without words)

   e. `function test_gapBackfillEntropyUnique_fuzz(uint256 vrfWord1, uint256 vrfWord2) public`:
      - Bound both words to [1, type(uint256).max]
      - Complete day 1, trigger day 2, stall + swap at 10-day gap
      - Resume with vrfWord2
      - Collect all gap day words: `uint256[] memory words; for d in gap range: words.push(game.rngWordForDay(d))`
      - Assert every word is nonzero
      - Assert every pair of words is distinct (double loop or sort-and-compare)
      - This proves keccak256(vrfWord, gapDay) produces unique per-day entropy

   f. `function test_indexLifecycleAcrossStall_fuzz(uint256 vrfWord1, uint256 vrfWord2) public`:
      - Bound both words to [1, type(uint256).max]
      - Record index before day 1
      - Complete day 1, record index after

      - Trigger day 2 VRF, coordinator swap, resume
      - Record final index
      - Assert final index - initial index == number of successful VRF requests (no double-increment, no skip)

IMPORTANT: Use absolute timestamps for day boundaries where needed. Deploy is at ts=86400 (day 1). Day N boundary is N * 86400.
