V37-006 (INFO): Gameover fallback uses prevrandao (1-bit L2 sequencer manipulation). _getHistoricalRngFallback mixes up to 5 committed VRF words with currentDay and block.prevrandao. On Base L2 sequencer controls prevrandao. Edge-of-edge case: gameover + VRF dead 3+ days. 5 VRF words provide 1280 bits entropy; prevrandao gives sequencer 2x advantage on binary outcome, not deterministic control. Documented trade-off in NatSpec lines 933-941. (STALL-06)

V37-007 (INFO): _getHistoricalRngFallback level-0 path returns prevrandao-only entropy. If game has zero completed days (no historical VRF words), combined==0 and output is keccak256(0, currentDay, prevrandao). At level 0 there are no player positions to manipulate. (STALL-06)
