**XCON-03: unwrapTo Stall Guard Verification**

1. Trace DegenerusStonk.unwrapTo() line 151:
   `if (block.timestamp - IDegenerusGame(ContractAddresses.GAME).lastVrfProcessed() > 20 hours) revert Unauthorized();`

2. Boundary analysis at exactly 20 hours:
   - At t = lastVrf + 72000 seconds (exactly 20h): `72000 > 72000` is FALSE. unwrapTo is ALLOWED.
   - At t = lastVrf + 72001 seconds (20h + 1s): `72001 > 72000` is TRUE. unwrapTo is BLOCKED.
   - This means there is a 1-second window at the exact 20h mark where unwrapTo is permitted AND governance is also active (vote() stall check: `< ADMIN_STALL_THRESHOLD` where ADMIN_STALL_THRESHOLD = 20h; at exactly 20h, `72000 < 72000` is FALSE, so vote proceeds).

3. Impact of the 1-second window:
   - Creator could call unwrapTo at exactly t=lastVrf+72000 to convert DGNRS to sDGNRS
   - This increases a recipient's sDGNRS balance
   - If the recipient then votes, their vote weight includes the newly received sDGNRS
   - BUT: sDGNRS goes to a specified recipient (not necessarily the creator), and the creator cannot vote with DGNRS
   - Severity: Informational/Low -- exploitable only if creator can time a transaction to the exact second, the recipient coordinates to vote immediately, and the amount is material relative to circulating supply
