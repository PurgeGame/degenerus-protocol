## Accomplishments
- Replaced 11 `price` storage reads across 4 modules (AdvanceModule, JackpotModule, LootboxModule, DegenerusGame) with `PriceLookupLib.priceForLevel` pure calls, eliminating SLOAD per read

- Split quest pricing: `handlePurchase` now receives separate `mintPrice` (purchaseLevel) and `levelQuestPrice` (level+1), ensuring level quest targets stay at next-level price during jackpot phase

## Decisions Made
- D-04: Price storage variable eliminated -- pricing is deterministic from level via PriceLookupLib pure function. Saves 1 SLOAD per price read and 1 SSTORE per level advance.
- D-05: Quest handlePurchase split into two prices -- `mintPrice` (purchaseLevel) for daily quest targets and `levelQuestPrice` (level+1) for level quest targets. During normal purchase phase both are identical. During jackpot phase, daily quests use current level's lower price while level quests maintain next-level higher price target.
