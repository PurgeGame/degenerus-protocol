Because the scheduled roll's outcome is computationally verifiable on-chain before execution (either because the "daily seed" is already known for the day, or by atomically simulating the block's roll in a smart contract), a player can preemptively evaluate the gamble. By selectively allowing the scheduled flush when their controlled affiliate wins, and front-running it with a manual flush (unsubscribing) when they lose, the player forces the protocol to always pay out the maximum of the two options.

2. Accumulate: Wallet A stays subscribed for the ~10-day epoch, accruing `affiliateBase`.
3. Option Evaluation: Once the window is eligible for settlement, Wallet A uses a smart contract to simulate the pending scheduled roll for the current block/day.

This churn loop systematically extracts an extra 18.75% of the total accrued affiliate base per cycle. As long as the market value of this 18.75% BURNIE bonus exceeds the gas cost of the unsub/resub transaction, the loop yields pure risk-free positive EV and actively steals from Upline 1 and 2.
