- Removed entire Phase 1 carryover ETH distribution state machine (~60 lines of multi-call loop code)

- Suppressed unused `carryoverSourceOffset` in ETH-path unpack call (still destructured and used in `payDailyJackpotCoinAndTickets` Phase 2)
- Changed `// Retrieve stored state from Phase 1` comment to `// Retrieve stored state from ETH phase` (no history in comments)
