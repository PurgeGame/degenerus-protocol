**PAY-07: Coinflip Deposit/Win/Loss Paths** (BurnieCoinflip:231-627)

The audit MUST cover:

1. **Deposit path**: Verify `creditFlip(player, amount)` and `creditFlipBatch(players, amounts)` correctly credit next-day coinflip wagers. Verify access control: only BurnieCoin contract can call these. Verify the credited amount is stored as a pending wager, NOT as a direct token transfer.

2. **VRF resolution**: Verify the coinflip outcome is determined by VRF randomness (50/50 chance). Verify the variable multiplier range (1.5x-2.5x per v1.1-burnie-coinflip.md). Verify win payout = `stake * (1 + rewardPercent/100)` where rewardPercent varies by multiplier tier.

3. **Win path**: Verify winning triggers BURNIE minting. Verify `mint(winner, payout)` where `payout = stake + reward`. Verify no double-payout path (stake is consumed on resolution, not claimable again).

4. **Loss path**: Verify losing permanently destroys the principal (no refund, no partial return). Verify WWXRP consolation prize is minted (1 WWXRP per loss day -- connects to PAY-18).

5. **Claim mechanism**: Verify `claimCoinflips(player, amount)` processes win/loss for each day in the claim window. Verify `claimCoinflipsFromBurnie(player, amount)` is called from BurnieCoin contract and routes to the same `_claimCoinflipsInternal` logic (Research Open Question 4). Verify both paths have identical protections.

6. **Claim window**: Verify COIN_CLAIM_FIRST_DAYS = 30 days for first-time claimants and COIN_CLAIM_DAYS = 90 days for returning claimants (CP-05 from research). Classify the asymmetry as INFO if not documented in natspec.

7. **Auto-rebuy carry**: Verify auto-rebuy carry calculations (CP-03 from research). Trace `_calcAutoRebuy` interaction. Verify the carry forward to next day's wager does not amplify extraction. Verify `ethSpent = baseTickets * ticketPrice` is correctly deducted. Verify bonus (130% normal, 145% afKing) is protocol-funded, not an accounting error.

**PAY-08: Coinflip Bounty System** (BurnieCoinflip:634-693 arming, 849-879 resolution)

The audit MUST cover:

1. **Bounty accumulation**: Verify 1000 BURNIE/day accumulation rate. Verify the accumulation mechanism and storage.

2. **Arming conditions**: Verify DGNRS gating: requires 50k BURNIE bet AND 20k DGNRS in pool. Verify these thresholds match the v1.1 spec.

3. **Resolution**: Verify half of accumulated bounty goes to winner as flip stake. Verify the resolution trigger and winner selection.

4. **Payout**: Verify bounty payout is credited via `creditFlip` (becomes next-day wager, subject to normal win/loss resolution).
