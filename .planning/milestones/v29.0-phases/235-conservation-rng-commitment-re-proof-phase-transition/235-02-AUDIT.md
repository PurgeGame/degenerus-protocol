## Methodology

BURNIE conservation is a mint/burn balance invariant: the cumulative sum of every `mintForGame(to, amount)` plus the constructor mint at `BurnieCoin.sol:213` plus every `vaultMintTo` allowance-spend must equal `balanceOf` across all holders plus every `_burn` call (`burnCoin` / `decimatorBurn` / `terminalDecimatorBurn` / `burnForCoinflip`). Any new mint site that bypasses the `mintForGame` gateway, or any new burn site that fails to decrement `balanceOf` + `_supply.totalSupply`, breaks the invariant.

**Per-Mint-Site Catalog** enumerates every function at HEAD that can increase either `_supply.totalSupply` (circulating BURNIE) or `_supply.vaultAllowance` (virtual vault escrow), and verifies each routes through one of three gateway patterns:

1. `mintForGame(address to, uint256 amount) external` at `BurnieCoin.sol:428-432` — the canonical mint gateway; caller-gated to `msg.sender == ContractAddresses.GAME || msg.sender == ContractAddresses.COINFLIP`; invokes `_mint(to, amount)`.
2. `vaultMintTo(address to, uint256 amount) external onlyVault` at `BurnieCoin.sol:517-529` — the vault-allowance consumer; caller-gated to `ContractAddresses.VAULT`; transfers allowance → totalSupply + `balanceOf[to]`.
3. `vaultEscrow(uint256 amount) external` at `BurnieCoin.sol:500-511` — the virtual-escrow allowance increaser; caller-gated to `GAME || VAULT`; bumps `_supply.vaultAllowance` only (no `totalSupply` delta).

Plus the one-time constructor seed at `BurnieCoin.sol:212-214` (`_mint(ContractAddresses.SDGNRS, 2_000_000 ether)`) which runs exactly once at deploy.

**Per-Burn-Site Catalog** enumerates every function at HEAD that can decrement either `_supply.totalSupply` or `_supply.vaultAllowance`:

1. `burnCoin(target, amount)` at `BurnieCoin.sol:537-543` — gameplay burn gateway; `onlyGame`; invokes `_burn(target, amount - consumed)`.
2. `decimatorBurn(player, amount)` at `BurnieCoin.sol:558-618` (**MODIFIED by 3ad0f8d3**) — decimator participation burn; no caller restriction (permissionless, keyed by operator approval); invokes `_burn(caller, amount - consumed)` at L578.
3. `terminalDecimatorBurn(player, amount)` at `BurnieCoin.sol:633-659` — terminal (death-bet) decimator burn; no caller restriction (operator approval); invokes `_burn(caller, amount - consumed)` at L650.
4. `burnForCoinflip(from, amount)` at `BurnieCoin.sol:419-422` — coinflip-deposit burn; caller-gated to `ContractAddresses.COINFLIP`; invokes `_burn(from, amount)`.
5. `_burn(VAULT, amount)` branch inside `_burn(from, amount)` at `BurnieCoin.sol:390-406` — drains `_supply.vaultAllowance` (not circulating supply) when `from == VAULT`; only reachable via external `vaultBurn` / `transfer(to=VAULT)` round-trips that first route through the caller-gated entry points.

**Quest Credit Algebra** walks the post-`f20a2b5e` + `d5284be5` `_purchaseFor` → `_callTicketPurchase` → `quests.handlePurchase(... burnieMintQty ...)` → quest-accumulator chain, proving (a) `burnieMintQty` passed to `handlePurchase` equals the accumulator value assigned inside `_callTicketPurchase`, (b) `d5284be5`'s scope is confined to the ETH parameter switch (`uint32 ethMintQty → uint256 ethFreshWei`), (c) `DegenerusQuests.handlePurchase` mints zero BURNIE (reward routing is `coinflip.creditFlip`, which is a stake credit, not a `burnie.mintForGame`).
