**Finding**

1. BURNIE seed emission is not conserved as claimed.  
Claim: `8M` seeded stake should have about `4M` EV, replacing the removed `2M + 2M` lumps.  
Code: seeds `200,000 BURNIE/day` for `20` days to both VAULT and sDGNRS, so `8M` total stake, but a winning coinflip mints `stake + stake * rewardPercent / 100`, not just stake. See [BurnieCoinflip.sol:154](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/BurnieCoinflip.sol:154), [BurnieCoinflip.sol:203](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/BurnieCoinflip.sol:203), [BurnieCoinflip.sol:541](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/BurnieCoinflip.sol:541), and [BurnieCoinflip.sol:861](/home/zak/Dev/PurgeGame/degenerus-audit/contracts/BurnieCoinflip.sol:861).  
Effect: normal-day expected rewardPercent is `96.85%`, so seeded stake EV is `0.5 * (1 + 0.9685) = 98.425%` of stake, or about `7.874M BURNIE` before any bonus-day uplift, not about `4M`. Survive-before-mint is true, but emission EV is nearly doubled versus the stated conservation target.
