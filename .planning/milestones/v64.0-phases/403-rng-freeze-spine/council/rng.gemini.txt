Based on the RNG-freeze spine correctness review for the v64 delta, here is the verification of the protocol's dominant invariants across the new and changed RNG consumers.

### RNG-Freeze Spine Correctness Review - v64 Delta

#### 1. Degenerette Box-Spin Seeds
*   **Location:** `contracts/modules/DegenerusGameDegeneretteModule.sol:926, 976, 1020`
*   **Verdict:** **FROZEN**
*   **Analysis:**
    *   **Commitment (RNG-01):** Players commit their `customTicket` and `heroQuadrant` in `placeDegeneretteBet` while `lootboxRngWordByIndex[index] == 0`. The bet is stored in `degeneretteBets`.
    *   **Reveal:** The bet is resolved in `resolveBets` only after `lootboxRngWordByIndex[index]` is non-zero. If the resolution awards a box, the "box-spin" (WWXRP/BURNIE/ETH) is triggered via `resolveLootboxDirect` using a seed derived from the same `rngWord` (tagged via `hash2`). Since the word was unknown at the moment of the bet placement, the outcome is frozen.
    *   **In-Window SLOADs (RNG-02):** The `activityScore` is snapshotted at placement. The `level` is read during resolution but only for ticket pricing (monotonic escalation), which prevents grinding.
    *   **One-Shot (RNG-03):** The `degeneretteBets` entry is deleted at the start of `resolveBets`. Box-spin resolvers are gated with `address(this) != GAME` to prevent direct module calls.

#### 2. Decimator Claim-Seed
*   **Location:** `contracts/modules/DegenerusGameDecimatorModule.sol:137, 308`
*   **Verdict:** **FROZEN**
*   **Analysis:**
    *   **Commitment (RNG-01):** Players commit their `bucket` and `subBucket` (deterministic) in `recordDecBurn` while the level is active and `decClaimRounds[lvl].poolWei == 0`.
    *   **Reveal:** `runDecimatorJackpot` is called during level advancement, revealing the `rngWord` and selecting winning subbuckets.
    *   **Claim Lootbox:** `claimDecimatorJackpot` uses the level's `rngWord` to resolve awarded lootboxes. Since the player committed their burn before the level advanced, the seed is unknown at commitment.
    *   **In-Window SLOADs (RNG-02):** `poolWei` and `totalBurn` are snapshotted in `decClaimRounds` during resolution. The `activityScore` for the lootbox is derived from the frozen `winBucket` (sealed at burn time).
    *   **One-Shot (RNG-03):** `e.claimed` is set to 1 before any value is credited.

#### 3. Redemption Lootbox Seed
*   **Location:** `contracts/StakedDegenerusStonk.sol:1003` & `contracts/modules/DegenerusGameLootboxModule.sol:926`
*   **Verdict:** **FROZEN**
*   **Analysis:**
    *   **Commitment (RNG-01):** Players commit burns in `_submitGamblingClaimFrom`. The contract enforces `game.rngWordForDay(currentPeriod) != 0` (preventing post-draw grinding of the current day's word), but the redemption/lootbox resolves on the **next** day's word (`day + 1`).
    *   **Reveal:** The `day + 1` word is drawn during the transition from `day` to `day + 1`.
    *   **Resolution:** `claimRedemption` reads the `day + 1` word to derive the lootbox entropy.
    *   **In-Window SLOADs (RNG-02):** `ethValueOwed`, `activityScore`, and `burnieEscrow` are snapshotted in `pendingRedemptions` at the moment of the burn.
    *   **One-Shot (RNG-03):** `pendingRedemptions[player][day]` is deleted at the start of `_claimRedemptionFor` (CEI).

### Summary of Focus Questions

1.  **Commitment-vs-reveal:** All paths (Degenerette, Decimator, Redemption) strictly isolate the commitment (input selection) from the reveal (VRF word generation).
2.  **In-window mutable SLOAD:** No player-controllable state mutations were found that could bias the RNG outcome. Mutable state like `level` only affects pricing/scaling in a deterministic, non-profitable way for delayers.
3.  **Zero-word / not-yet-fulfilled:** All consumers gate on non-zero words. The `sDGNRS` `BurnsBlockedBeforeDailyRng` fix correctly prevents the redemption pre-draw grind.
4.  **One-shot:** All resolvers follow the Checks-Effects-Interactions (CEI) pattern, clearing or flagging state before external/value calls.
5.  **Delegatecall guard:** Box-spin resolvers in `DegeneretteModule` are correctly gated. Other modules rely on `msg.sender` authorization to interact with `coin` and `sdgnrs`, which fails if the module is called directly.

**Conclusion:** The RNG-freeze invariant holds across the v64 delta. The result is **CLEAN**.
