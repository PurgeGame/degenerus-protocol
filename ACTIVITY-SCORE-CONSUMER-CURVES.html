Activity-Score Consumer Curves · v70

Every reward that reads the player activity score maps it through a 3-segment piecewise curve: a steep early ramp to vA at the old cap K (90% of the gain), a shallow leg to vB at score 500 (98%), then a near-flat crawl to MAX at score 30,000 (100%), flat beyond. MIN and MAX are byte-identical to before the reshape — only the ramp shape changed, reserving the top of each curve for long quest streaks. Curves computed with the exact contract integer math.
