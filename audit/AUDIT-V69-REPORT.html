The headline — the 436 IMPL migration was INCOMPLETE

The 436 IMPL collapsed _playerActivityScoreAt to the point domain (1 pt = 100 bps) and migrated the score producer plus three consumer families, but the 435 design-lock's TABLE-A consumer inventory was not exhaustive: 6 consumer sites in 4 files still read the score as basis points. Once playerActivityScore returned points (~100x smaller), those sites silently mis-scaled. The council confirmed (three independent confirmations, 0 divergence) all 4 findings REAL, the 6-site list EXHAUSTIVE, and every fix scale-invariant with no new overflow. All four fixes are the same mechanical /100 constant migration (plus one (points*100)/3 re-scale) already approved for the terminal-decimator path in 436.

MIGRATE-01 — HIGH — FLIP regular decimatorBurn bonus ~100x nullified, adverse-to-player (FIXED c297893a)
FLIP.sol regular (non-terminal) decimatorBurn kept the bps-domain cap 23_500, the bonusBps/3 multiplier, and a bucket reduction divided by 23_500 — all now fed a points-domain score. The bonus collapsed ~100x (235-pt input gave 1.008x instead of 1.78x), under-paying every regular decimator burner. Fix: cap -> 235, multiplier (bonusPoints*100)/3, bucket denominator /235, param rename. Same re-scale the IMPL applied to the terminal path; the regular path was the missed sibling.
MIGRATE-02 — HIGH — DegenerusAffiliate lootbox taper never fires, value-leak (FIXED c297893a)
DegenerusAffiliate.sol lootbox taper compared a points-domain score against bps thresholds 10_000 / 25_500. With the ~100x-smaller score the anti-farming taper never engaged -> 100% affiliate over-payout. Fix: thresholds -> 100 / 255 + taper doc-comment to points.
MIGRATE-03 — MEDIUM — century quantity bonus collapses ~100x at two sites, adverse-to-player (FIXED c297893a)
DegenerusGameMintModule.sol and GameAfkingModule.sol both compute qty * min(score, 30_500) / 30_500; the points-domain numerator shrank ~100x so the bonus degenerated to ~qty/100. Fix: denominator 30_500 -> 305 at both sites.
MIGRATE-04 — MEDIUM — DecimatorModule _minScoreForBucket bimodal EV, favourable-to-actor (FIXED c297893a)
_minScoreForBucket kept the bps cap 23_500 against the points-domain score, producing a bimodal 90% / 145% decimator-claim EV on every win (graduated EV ladder collapsed to two outcomes). Fix: cap -> 235.

Remediation c297893a (8 .sol files incl. getter/interface natspec to points) + tests fa21e39e (new V69ConsumerMigrationFixes.t.sol, 5 pure-math fails-without/passes-with proofs + V56/V61/DegeneretteHeroScore re-pins). USER-approved.
