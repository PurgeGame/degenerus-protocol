## 1. Game Structure

Degenerus is an on-chain ETH game with repeating **levels**. Each level has two phases:

```
PURCHASE PHASE (players buy tickets) → JACKPOT PHASE (5-day prize draws) → next level
```

Players buy **tickets** (and other products) during the purchase phase. ETH is split across prize pools. When enough ETH accumulates, the level advances to a 5-day jackpot phase where prizes are distributed to ticketholders based on ticket traits. Then the next level begins.

The game runs until a **death clock** expires (120 days of inactivity at level 1+, 365 days at level 0), triggering a terminal distribution.

### Key Constants
- BPS denominator: **10,000** (all percentages in basis points)
- BURNIE unit: **1,000 BURNIE** (PRICE_COIN_UNIT)
- Jackpot reset time: **22:57 UTC** daily
- Bootstrap prize pool: **50 ETH** (level 0 target)

## 2. ETH Entry Paths (9 Purchase Types)

Every ETH entering the game is split across pools. The split ratios differ by purchase type.

| Purchase Type | Cost | Key Pool Split |
|---------------|------|----------------|
| **Ticket** | Level-dependent (0.01-0.24 ETH) | 10% future / 90% next (base split via PURCHASE_TO_FUTURE_BPS=1000); long-run averages shift to ~55% future / ~30% next after drawdown rebalancing at level transitions |
| **Lootbox** | 2x ticket price | Higher future% than tickets, BURNIE bonus on purchase |
| **Whale Bundle** | 2.4 ETH (L0-3) / 4 ETH (L4+) | 40+ tickets + future-level freeze, 1% DGNRS from whale pool |
| **Lazy Pass** | Sum of 10 level prices | 10-level coverage, tickets at each level |
| **Deity Pass** | 24 + T(k) ETH (quadratic) | 30/70 next/future (L0), 5/95 (L1+), boon drops |
| **Degenerette** | 0.01 ETH min bet | Side-game wager, 90-110% ROI based on activity |
| **BURNIE Tickets** | 1,000 BURNIE burned | Virtual ETH calculated, no actual ETH enters |

### Price Curve (Ticket Cost by Level)
```
L0-4:   0.01 ETH     L30-59:  0.08 ETH     L90-99:  0.16 ETH
L5-9:   0.02 ETH     L60-89:  0.12 ETH     x00:     0.24 ETH
L10-29: 0.04 ETH     (pattern repeats every 100 levels with x00 milestone at 0.24)
```

## 3. Pool Architecture

ETH flows through a 4-pool lifecycle:

```
futurePool → nextPool → currentPool → claimablePool
    ↑            ↑           ↑              ↑
 accumulates   becomes    becomes        winners
 from splits   current    claimable      withdraw
               at level   at level
               advance    advance
```

- **futurePool**: Long-term accumulator. Majority of ETH lands here. Source for daily drip, BAF, and decimator.
- **nextPool**: Short-term. Becomes currentPool when level advances. Purchase target threshold.
- **currentPool**: Active jackpot pool for the current level's 5-day draws.
- **claimablePool**: Finalized payouts. Winners withdraw from here.

**Level advancement trigger:** `nextPool >= levelPrizePool[level - 1]` (except x00 levels which use `futurePool / 3`)

**Freeze mechanics:** During jackpot phase, pools are frozen. New purchases go to pending accumulators (packed uint128+uint128) and merge on unfreeze.

**Insurance skim:** At each level transition, 1% of nextPool is skimmed to the yieldAccumulator before nextPool becomes currentPool (`INSURANCE_SKIM_BPS = 100`, AdvanceModule.sol:107). This builds a growing terminal insurance reserve.

> **Source:** `v1.1-pool-architecture.md` for packed storage layout and transition triggers

## 4. Prize Distribution

### Purchase Phase (Daily)

Every day during purchase phase:

- **ETH drip:** 1% of futurePool → 25% as ETH to trait winners, 75% as lootbox tickets to nextPool
- **BURNIE jackpot:** 0.5% of previous level's target pool → distributed as coinflip credits to near-future (75%) and far-future (25%) ticketholders

### Jackpot Phase (5 Days)

When level advances, a 5-day draw sequence begins:

| Day | Pool Slice | Bucket Split |
|-----|-----------|--------------|
| 1-4 | 6-14% of currentPool (random) | 20% each to 4 trait buckets |
| 5 | 100% remainder (~66%) | 60% / 13% / 13% / 13% |

Each bucket awards **25/15/8/1 winners** (base, scales 1-6.67x with pool size). Winners get ETH directly. 20% of daily budget converts to lootbox tickets (2x over-collateralized).

**Trait matching:** Tickets have 4 traits (3 symbols + 1 color). Each day's winning trait is drawn from VRF entropy. Matching tickets enter that day's bucket.

> **Source:** `v1.1-jackpot-phase-draws.md` for payout multiplier tiers (50%/150%/78-115%), deity virtual entries, compressed jackpot mechanics

### Transition Jackpots (Level Boundaries)

**BAF (every 10 levels):** 10% of futurePool distributed (20% at level 50 and x00). Prize split: 10% top BAF bettor, 5% top coinflip bettor, 5% random 3rd/4th BAF, 10% far-future ticketholders (two independent 5% draws), 70% scatter (45% 1st place / 25% 2nd place across 50 sampling rounds). Large winners get 50% ETH / 50% lootbox.

**Decimator (every 10 levels, offset by 5):** Players burn BURNIE to enter a competitive pool. 10% of futurePool allocated (30% at x00). Pro-rata payout based on BURNIE burned. Multiplier tiers up to 200,000 BURNIE cap. Note: level 95 (and x95) is excluded from the normal decimator schedule (`lvl % 100 != 95`), so the decimator fires at levels 5, 15, 25, 35, 45, 55, 65, 75, 85, 100, 105, etc.

> **Critical:** At x00 levels, BAF (20%) + Decimator (30%) = **50% of futurePool** distributed in a single transition.

> **Source:** `v1.1-transition-jackpots.md` for bucket mechanics, multiplier cap splitting, claim expiry

## 5. BURNIE Token Economy

BURNIE is a secondary token used for wagering, side-games, and competitive entry.

### Earning Paths
- **Coinflip wins:** 50/50 odds, ~1.97x average payout (~1.575% house edge)
- **Lootbox bonuses:** 58-590% of purchase value (random tier)
- **Quest rewards:** 100-200 BURNIE per daily quest
- **Bounty flips:** 1,000 BURNIE/day accumulates, 50% to winner

### Burn Sinks
- **Coinflip losses:** 50% of outcomes permanently burn the stake
- **Decimator entry:** Burn BURNIE to compete for ETH share
- **BURNIE ticket purchases:** 1,000 BURNIE burned per ticket

### Supply Dynamics
- Initial vault: 2,000,000 BURNIE
- **No hard cap** on supply (coinflip mints bypass vault)
- Invariant: `totalSupply + vaultAllowance = supplyIncUncirculated`
- Vault transfers are supply exits (not permanent burns)

> **Source:** `v1.1-burnie-coinflip.md` for payout tiers, recycling, boon effects; `v1.1-burnie-supply.md` for full supply tracking

## 6. Activity Score System

Activity score (0-30,500 BPS) determines lootbox EV, degenerette ROI, and affiliate payouts. It's the central incentive mechanism.

### Components

| Component | Max BPS | How |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| Mint streak | 5,000 | Consecutive levels purchased (50 BPS/level, max 100) |
| Mint count | 2,500 | Purchases in current level (25 BPS per mint/level) |
| Quest streak | 10,000 | Consecutive daily quests (100 BPS/day, max 100 days) |
| Whale bundle | 4,000 | Bundle purchase bonus (replaces deity if lower) |
| Deity pass | 8,000 | Flat deity bonus + floors (streak 50%, count 25%) |
| Affiliate bonus | variable | min(ETH from prev 5 levels / 1 ETH, 50) * 100 BPS |

**Non-deity max:** ~21,500 BPS | **Deity max:** ~25,500 BPS | **Degenerette max:** 30,500 BPS

### Lootbox EV Curve
```
Activity 0      → 80% EV (negative expected value)
Activity 6,000  → 100% EV (breakeven)
Activity 25,500 → 135% EV (positive expected value)
```

> **Source:** `v1.1-endgame-and-activity.md` for piecewise EV formulas, degenerette ROI curves, full Solidity for `_playerActivityScore`

## 7. Death Clock & Endgame

- **Level 0:** 365-day timeout from deploy
- **Level 1+:** 120-day timeout from last level advancement
- **Imminent:** 5 days before timeout — visual warning
- **Distress:** 6 hours before timeout — 25% bonus tickets on purchases
- **Safety valve:** If purchase target met during distress, levelStartTime resets (extends clock)

### Terminal Distribution (gameOver)
When death clock expires:
1. Final jackpot at **lvl+1** (NOT current level — only future ticketholders win)
2. Decimator payout (10% of remaining) — 100% ETH (no lootbox)
3. Deity pass refund (20 ETH/pass, levels 0-9 only, budget-capped)
4. **Final sweep** (30 days after gameOver): unclaimed ETH split 50% to vault, 50% to sDGNRS burn-for-backing

> **Critical pitfall:** Terminal jackpot targets **lvl+1**, not current level. Only players with whale bundles, lazy passes, or lootbox tickets at the next level can win.

## 8. Reward Modifiers

### DGNRS / sDGNRS Token (Dual-Contract Architecture)
- 1 trillion total supply, split across two contracts:
  - **sDGNRS (StakedDegenerusStonk):** Soulbound — no transfer function. Holds all ETH/stETH/BURNIE reserves and 5 reward pools. Game distributes sDGNRS to players from pools.
  - **DGNRS (DegenerusStonk):** Transferable ERC20 wrapper. Creator's 20% allocation. Full transfer/transferFrom/approve. Secondary market possible.
- Pool splits (of sDGNRS total supply): Creator→DGNRS 20%, Whale 10%, Affiliate 35%, Lootbox 20%, Reward 5%, Earlybird 10%
- Burn-for-backing (either token): proportional claim on `(ethBal + stethBal + claimable) * burnAmount / totalSupply` from sDGNRS reserves
- DGNRS burn() delegates to sDGNRS.burn(); unwrapTo() converts DGNRS back to soulbound sDGNRS

### Deity Passes (Premium, 32 max)
- Pricing: 24 + T(k) ETH (triangular numbers). Range: 24 ETH (first) to 520 ETH (32nd). Cumulative: 6,224 ETH.
- 31 boon types across 10 categories (coinflip/lootbox/purchase/decimator/whale/deity/activity/pass discounts)
- +80% activity score, jackpot virtual entries (floor(2% of bucket, min 2))
- **Boon overwrite pitfall:** New boon replaces existing — 5% can overwrite 25%

### Affiliate System (3-Tier)
- Direct affiliate: 25% of fresh ETH (L0-3), 20% (L4+), 5% of recycled
- Upline1: 20% of direct's share. Upline2: 4% of direct's share.
- Lootbox activity taper: 25-100% payout based on activity score (10K-25.5K range)
- DGNRS claim: 5% of affiliate pool per level (not reserved — sequential depletion)
- All rewards credited as coinflip stakes via `creditFlip()`

### afKing Mode
- Enhanced auto-rebuy mode with a 5-level lock (`AFKING_LOCK_LEVELS = 5`). Once enabled, cannot be disabled until 5 levels have passed.
- Forces auto-rebuy on for both ETH and BURNIE, clamps take-profit minimums to 5 ETH / 20,000 BURNIE.
- +45% auto-rebuy BURNIE bonus (14,500 BPS vs normal 13,000 BPS). Deity pass holders get an additional per-level coinflip recycling bonus (up to 1.5% max).

### Quest System
- Slot 0: Fixed MINT_ETH quest, 100 BURNIE reward (forces daily ETH activity)
- Slot 1: Weighted random from 9 types, 200 BURNIE reward
- Streak: +1% activity per consecutive day, max 100% (10,000 BPS) — largest single activity component

### stETH Yield
- Admin stakes excess ETH as stETH via Lido (reserve = claimablePool minus vault/sDGNRS claimable, since those accept stETH)
- **Yield distribution (per level transition):** 23% sDGNRS claimable, 23% vault claimable, 46% to segregated yield accumulator, ~8% buffer
- **x00 milestone release:** 50% of accumulator → futurePrizePool (enters before keep-roll, so only the kept fraction reaches currentPrizePool), 50% retained as terminal insurance
- Accumulator grows monotonically — every level survived strengthens terminal incentive
- Payout order: Players get ETH first (stETH fallback). Vault/sDGNRS get stETH first.

## 9. Critical Agent Pitfalls

These are the most common reasoning errors. Internalize before writing about the protocol.

1. **Terminal jackpot targets lvl+1, not current level** — only next-level ticketholders win the final payout
2. **Lootbox 2x over-collateralization** — full budget goes to pool backing, but only 50% determines ticket count
3. **BURNIE coinflip losses are permanent burns** — 50% of all stakes destroyed forever
4. **Decimator claim expiry** — when next decimator resolves, all prior unclaimed rewards expire permanently
5. **Deity boon overwrite can be a downgrade** — 5% boon replaces a 25% boon with no confirmation
6. **Affiliate DGNRS "5% per level" is not reserved** — multiple claimants sequentially deplete the pool
7. **PPM vs BPS confusion** — whale bundle DGNRS rewards use PPM (1,000,000 scale), deity pass rewards use BPS (10,000)
8. **x00 levels drain 50% of futurePool** — BAF (20%) + Decimator (30%) fire simultaneously
9. **Quest slot 0 is mandatory before slot 1** — forces daily ETH purchase as prerequisite for bonus quests
10. **Three-tier jackpot compression** — `compressedJackpotFlag`: 0=normal (5-day draws), 1=compressed (3-day draws), 2=turbo (1-day draw). Compressed/turbo modes merge multiple logical days into fewer physical days at higher per-day rates, not by doubling BPS
11. **Distress bonus is proportional** — only the fraction of tickets bought during distress gets the 25% boost, not all tickets
12. **stETH has 1-2 wei rounding errors** — handled by retry logic but affects exact calculations

## 10. Key Economic Relationships

```
                    ┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
                    │           ETH ENTERS (purchases)        │
                    └───────┬──────────┬──────────┬───────────┘
                            │          │          │
                       futurePool   nextPool   currentPool
                            │          │          │
                   ┌────────┴───┐      │     (jackpot draws)
                   │            │      │          │
              daily drip    BAF/Dec   level     winners
              (1%/day)     (10-30%)  advance    withdraw
                   │            │      │
              lootbox+ETH   ETH+loot  nextPool→currentPool
                                      futurePool→nextPool (15% drawdown)
```

**Player incentive alignment:** Activity score is the fulcrum. High activity → better lootbox EV (up to 135%), better degenerette ROI, higher affiliate payouts. The quest streak (10,000 BPS max) is the single largest component, forcing daily engagement. Deity passes amplify all returns but cost 24-520 ETH each.

**BURNIE economy loop:** Earn BURNIE through coinflips/quests/lootboxes → burn in decimator for ETH share → or convert to tickets. Coinflip has ~1.575% house edge, making it slightly negative EV but high variance. The vault provides initial liquidity; ongoing supply is uncapped through coinflip mints.

**Endgame pressure:** Death clock creates urgency. As timeout approaches, rational players rush purchases for distress bonuses and to extend the clock. Terminal distribution rewards forward-looking players (lvl+1 ticketholders) over current-level participants.
