Confirmed findings — both fixed in-milestone

MIDRNG-02 — MEDIUM — LR_MID_DAY latch leak (FIXED 73eb242a)
The mid-day lootbox latch was released only by the same-day drain block; a mid-day ticket batch whose word arrives fine but whose drain crosses the day boundary completes on the new-day gate and leaves the latch stuck — permanently reverting requestLootboxRng (the mid-day fast path). Reachable by a benign keeper-timing race. The data plane was sound throughout; control-latch leak only. NET-2 found it with forge probes (the external council REFUTED it). Fix releases the latch on the new-day drain (guarded, non-regressive) + regression test_midDayLatch_clearsOnCrossDayDrain (pass-with-fix / fail-without). USER-approved.
DELEGATE-FIND-01 — LOW — direct-call ETH-trap (FIXED 095a7ac9)
Four payable delegatecall-only entrypoints lacked an address(this)==GAME guard → a direct call with ETH traps the caller's own value (no Game-state corruption, no drain). Codex-unique. Fixed with the existing guard idiom.
