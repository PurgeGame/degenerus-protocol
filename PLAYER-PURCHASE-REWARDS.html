0 How buying works — units, currencies & price

Two things you need before anything else: what a "ticket" actually is, and how much one costs at your level.

The unit: 1 ticket = 4 entries = 1 price

  • Every whole ticket mints across 4 trait quadrants → it places 4 jackpot entries into the prize buckets. More entries in a bucket = higher chance to win that bucket's jackpot.
  • One whole ticket always costs exactly one mint price for the current level. You can buy fractional tickets; minimum total buy-in is 0.0025 ETH.
  • A lootbox is not a fixed item — you choose how much ETH to throw in (minimum 0.01 ETH). See §9.

Mint price by level 0.01 → 0.24 ETH

Price is taken at your purchase level and climbs in bands, resetting each 100-level century with a spike at every x00 milestone.

Many rewards below scale with your Activity Score — a personal multiplier built from your mint streak, mint count, quest streak, affiliate volume, and any passes you hold. Higher score = better lootbox EV, better Degenerette RTP, and bigger airdrops. Where a reward depends on it, it's flagged.

1 Core mint — buying tickets with ETH

The main event: purchase(). Pay ETH (and/or claimable) for tickets, optionally bundling a lootbox.

What every ETH mint gives you

  • tickets Jackpot entries — 4 per whole ticket, queued into the trait buckets for your level. These are your jackpot & bingo lottery tickets.
  • BURNIE Flip credit on every buy — roughly 10% of the ticket's coin value (≈100 BURNIE per ticket-price), credited as a Coinflip stake. Buys of 10+ tickets get a bonus on top.
  • BURNIE Spend-it-all bonus — if you pay almost entirely from claimable (leaving less than one ticket), you get an extra +10% BURNIE flip bonus.
  • Activity Score progress — completing the daily mint quest extends your mint streak (+1% per consecutive level, up to +50%) and ticks your mint count (up to +25%).
  • lootbox If you allocated ≥0.01 ETH, a lootbox is queued and auto-opens (§9).
  • credit Presale-box credit — while presale is open, you bank 25% of your ETH spend as credit toward presale boxes (§3).

Situational bonuses

All ETH you spend on tickets flows into the prize pools (≈90% to the next jackpot, ≈10% to the future pool). Lootbox ETH is rake-free — 100% goes to prize pools, no house cut.

2 Coin mint — buying tickets with BURNIE

purchaseCoin() — spend your BURNIE coin instead of ETH. It's a pure coin sink, so the reward profile is leaner.

BURNIE-only mint 1,000 BURNIE / ticket

You still get

  • The full 4 jackpot entries per ticket — same lottery participation as an ETH mint.
  • A BURNIE quest-completion reward if your buy completes the daily coin-mint quest.

You do NOT get

  • No lootbox, no affiliate rewards, no Activity-Score boost, no presale credit, no x00 bonus.
  • No ETH enters the prize pools — the BURNIE is simply burned.

Blocked once the game is in a "game-over-possible" state. Use it to convert coin into jackpot entries, not to chase the side rewards.

3 Presale boxes

A presale-only product. You bank credit by minting during presale, then redeem it 1:1 for boxes that roll into BURNIE, DGNRS, or a dud.

Buy a presale box — buyPresaleBox()

Pay: ETH (wallet first, claimable for the rest)
Minimum: 0.01 ETH
Hard-gated 1:1 by your presale-box credit
Lifetime cap: 50 ETH of boxes total

The box is queued (frozen against the RNG) and opened later. It rolls into one of:

OutcomeChanceWhat you get
BURNIE50%A large BURNIE payout — this branch averages ≈400% of the box's ETH value
DGNRS40%DGNRS tokens on a declining tier curve (3.0× → 1.0× as more boxes sell)
WWXRP10%1 WWXRP joke token (a dud)

The single box that pushes the total to the 50 ETH cap also sweeps the entire remaining DGNRS presale pool to that buyer — on top of its own roll.

Where presale credit comes from

  • 25% of every ETH mint + lootbox spend during presale is banked as credit.
  • Afking (auto-buy) subscriptions also accrue credit from their pending BURNIE.
  • Combo buy — buyLootboxAndPresaleBox() mints tickets+lootbox with fresh ETH (earning fresh credit) and immediately spends that credit on a presale box, all on one shared RNG draw.

Once the 50 ETH cap is hit, presale latches closed and any overpaid ETH is refunded.

4 Whale bundle

purchaseWhaleBundle() — a 100-level mega-package: a stat freeze plus a flood of tickets across the next 100 levels.

Whale bundle 2.4 ETH (early) · 4 ETH (standard) / unit

Quantity 1–100. Early price (2.4 ETH) applies through level 4; 4 ETH after. A whale-boon discount can shave 10% / 20% / 35% off the first bundle.

  • stats 100-level stat freeze + level-count boost, and your LEVEL mint streak front-loaded by the freeze.
  • tickets Jackpot tickets across 100 levels — 40 × quantity per level for early levels (up to level 10), then 2 × quantity per level for the rest.
  • lootbox A lootbox worth 10% of the price paid.
  • DGNRS 1% of the Whale pool per bundle paid to you. (Your affiliate gets 0.1%, their upline 0.02% — see §8.)
  • +40% Activity Score the whole time the freeze is active.
  • Curse immunity from the stale-cashout curse while your freeze is active.
  • credit 25% presale-box credit while presale is open.

5 Lazy pass

purchaseLazyPass() — a smaller 10-level pass for set-and-forget players.

Lazy pass 0.24 ETH (early) · 10-level price (later)

At levels 0–2 it's a flat 0.24 ETH of benefit (overpay converts to bonus tickets). At level 3+ the price is the sum of the next 10 levels' ticket prices. A lazy-pass boon can discount it 10% / 25% / 50%.

  • stats A 10-level stat freeze + level-count boost, LEVEL streak front-loaded.
  • tickets 4 tickets per level × 10 levels (plus bonus tickets from any early-level overpay).
  • lootbox A lootbox worth 10% of the benefit value.
  • +10% Activity Score while the freeze is active.
  • credit 25% presale-box credit while presale is open.

Buyable only at certain levels (0–2, the x9 and x0 levels, an x00 in its purchase phase, or with a boon). Blocked if you already hold a Deity pass. Renew only when ≤7 freeze levels remain.

6 Deity pass

purchaseDeityPass(symbolId) — the apex item. A soulbound NFT that claims one of 32 symbols and pays out forever.

Deity pass 24 ETH → 520 ETH (escalating)

Price = 24 ETH + a triangular escalation by how many passes already sold. First pass 24 ETH; the 32nd (last) ≈520 ETH. Boon discount 10% / 20% / 35%. One per player, 32 total — one per symbol.

  • NFT A soulbound Deity NFT (tokenId = your chosen symbol; cannot be transferred or sold).
  • tickets Whale-equivalent tickets across 100 levels (40/level early, 2/level after).
  • ∞ entries Perpetual virtual jackpot entries for your symbol's trait buckets — automatically, every single jackpot, for as long as the pass exists. Gold tier gives 1; common tiers give more, scaling with bucket size.
  • lootbox A lootbox worth 10% of price.
  • DGNRS 5% of the Whale pool paid to you. (Affiliate 0.5%, upline 0.1% — §8.)
  • +80% Activity Score — always on (not tied to a freeze). The single biggest score boost in the game.
  • Curse immunity, plus you can issue 3 boons per day to other players and smite rivals (§ below).
Boons (your gift): 3 per day, each to a different player. Rolls a benefit from a weighted menu — coinflip/lootbox/purchase discounts, decimator boosts, pass discounts, even a rare whale-pass grant. Deity-granted boons are valid only that day.
Smite (your weapon): burn 200 BURNIE to stack a +2 curse on a target (caps at 10), dropping their Activity Score by 1% per point. Active afkers are immune; anyone can clear a curse for 100 BURNIE, and any real buy self-clears it.

If the game ends early (before level 10), each deity is refunded what they paid, capped at 20 ETH (and clamped to the available budget).

7 Whale pass — earned, not bought

There's no "buy a whale pass" button. It's a prize you accumulate in half-passes and then claim.

How to earn it & what it's worth

  • Each half-pass = 1 ticket/level × 100 levels (a full pass = 2 halves = 200 tickets over 100 levels).
  • Big lootbox / jackpot wins convert into half-passes (≈ win value ÷ 2.25 ETH).
  • WWXRP The first WWXRP Degenerette jackpot in each 10-level bracket grants 1 half-pass — the main reason WWXRP is worth anything.
  • Claim everything you've banked with claimWhalePass(): it queues your half-passes as tickets/level across 100 levels and applies whale-pass stats.

8 Affiliate rewards

Every address is already an affiliate. Refer buyers, earn a cut of their spend in BURNIE; optionally share a kickback back to them.

Getting set up

  • Every wallet has an automatic default code (0% kickback) — no transaction needed.
  • Want a custom code and/or to give buyers a discount? createAffiliateCode(code, kickback%) — kickback 0–25%, permanent and first-come-first-served.
  • A buyer is bound to you the first time they purchase with your code. Self-referral is blocked; a blank/invalid code locks that buyer to the protocol vault.

What the affiliate earns per referred purchase

Paid as BURNIE flip credit, as a % of the buyer's spend (converted to BURNIE):

Buyer paid with…Game levelAffiliate rate
Fresh ETH0–325%
Fresh ETH4+20%
Recycled (claimable) ETHany5%

Each purchase's reward goes to one winner by a weighted roll across three tiers, plus a quest bonus to the winner:

You (direct affiliate) — 75% odds
Your upline (U1) — 20% odds
Their upline (U2) — 5% odds

High-volume affiliates have the fresh-ETH reward tapered down (toward a 25% floor) once their activity score is very high. The afking auto-buy path pays the same 75/20/5 split, but deterministically every time instead of by a roll.

What the buyer gets (kickback)

  • If your code sets a kickback (0–25%), that slice of your reward is carved out and returned to the buyer as BURNIE flip credit. Default codes = 0%, so buyers only get a discount on a code that opted in.
  • The kickback comes out of the affiliate's share — it doesn't reduce their leaderboard score.

DGNRS & leaderboard rewards

  • sDGNRS claimAffiliateDgnrs() — once per level, claim your share of a 5%-of-pool DGNRS allocation, proportional to your referral volume. Needs ≥10 ETH of referral volume (waived if you hold a Deity pass). Deity holders also get a +20% of score BURNIE bonus (capped at 5 ETH-equiv).
  • sDGNRS Top affiliate each level gets an extra 1% of the Affiliate pool.
  • Your recent affiliate volume also adds up to +50% to your own mint trait rolls.

9 Lootboxes

You choose the ETH amount; the EV is gated by your Activity Score, then it rolls one of six outcomes — three of which play out as live Degenerette spins (§10) sourced from the box.

The roll

Effective value is scaled by Activity Score first: 90% EV at 0% score → 100% at 60% → up to 145% at max (benefit capped at 10 ETH per level). ~10% is split off to fund a boon draw. Boxes over 0.5 ETH split into two independent rolls.

OutcomeChanceWhat you get
Tickets40%Tickets at a future level — budget ≈197% of value, then a variance tier from 0.30× up to a rare 6.0×
DGNRS15%DGNRS from the lootbox pool (rare tiers pay dramatically more)
WWXRP spin15%A 1-WWXRP Degenerette spin — variance instead of a flat token, plus the rare jackpot whale half-pass
BURNIE15%Large BURNIE payout — 58–130% of value usually, or a rare 307–590%
BURNIE spins ×310%The would-be BURNIE split across three spins, then double-or-nothing on one survival coinflip
ETH spin5%An ETH Degenerette spin — pays claimable ETH plus a recirculated bonus box (recirc boxes award tickets here instead)

The spin rolls (WWXRP / BURNIE×3 / ETH) are real Degenerette spins scored by your Activity Score — same expected value as the flat awards they replaced, with added variance and reel feel. A ticket roll that rounds to zero pays a 1 WWXRP consolation. The ~10% boon budget can also drop coinflip/lootbox/purchase discounts, decimator boosts, pass discounts, activity boosts, a quest shield, or — rarely — a whale pass.

10 Degenerette — symbol-roll bets

placeDegeneretteBet() — the in-game roulette. Bet a currency, score 0–9, get paid off one of five tables.

Stakes & limits

Payouts

  • RTP scales with Activity Score: 90% at 0% → up to 99.9% at max. ETH bets get +5% bonus EV on top, concentrated in the top scores.
  • Top jackpot (score 9) pays from ~107,000× up to ~209,000× your bet, depending on how many gold symbols your pick has.
  • Big ETH wins pay partly in ETH and partly converted into a lootbox (and each ETH win is capped at 10% of the prize pool, the rest spilling to a lootbox).
  • sDGNRS ETH bets scoring 7+ also award sDGNRS (4% / 8% / 15% per ETH for scores 7 / 8 / 9).
  • whale pass The first WWXRP bet to hit the jackpot in each 10-level bracket grants a whale half-pass.

12 The tokens & burns at a glance

What the four tokens are, how you get them, and what they're for.

BURNIE  Burnie Coin

In-game coin. Earned from mint rewards, coinflip wins, Degenerette, and the permissionless mintBurnie() keeper bounty. Spent on coin mints, coinflip, Degenerette, smites, and the decimator "death bet" jackpot (min 1,000 BURNIE).

DGNRS / sDGNRS

Reward token (DGNRS) and its soulbound, asset-backed stake (sDGNRS). Earned from lootboxes, Degenerette, presale boxes, and affiliate claims. Redeemable for a proportional share of the ETH + stETH + BURNIE backing — deterministically post-game, or via a 25%–175% gamble roll (100% EV) while the game is live.

WWXRP  (parody)

An explicit joke token, intrinsically worthless. You get it from lootbox duds and coinflip losses. Its only real value: it's a valid Degenerette bet currency, and a WWXRP jackpot is the rare path to a whale half-pass.

Passes & NFTs

Whale bundle & lazy pass = timed stat freezes + ticket floods. Deity pass = soulbound NFT with perpetual jackpot entries + always-on score boost + boon/smite powers. Whale pass = earned ticket multiplier claimed in half-pass units.
